# 15-Day Plan: First Deposits for Pool Installation

**Goal**: Collect 50% deposits from customers for spring/summer pool installations

## Executive Summary

Tri-State Aquatic Solutions is expanding from pool maintenance (30 pools) into fiberglass pool installation, targeting the affluent Main Line PA and Northern Delaware markets. This plan focuses on rapid go-to-market execution to secure deposits within 15 days.

**Key Advantages**:
- Existing customer relationships (30 pool maintenance clients)
- Local presence in Hockessin, DE
- Established reputation in the market
- Underserved fiberglass pool market (competitors focus on gunite)

## DEPOSIT STRUCTURE (PA Compliant)

### Legal Maximum
- 33% of total contract price
- Plus cost of special-order materials (itemized separately)

### Recommended Structure
| Milestone | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Deposit (contract signing) | 25-30% |
| Materials order (itemized) | As quoted |
| Excavation start | 20% |
| Pool set | 20% |
| Completion | 5-10% |

### Example: $60,000 Pool
- Deposit: $18,000 (30%)
- Materials (pool shell): $15,000
- Excavation: $12,000
- Pool set: $12,000
- Completion: $3,000

## RISK MITIGATION

### If Registration is Delayed
- Continue consultations and relationship building
- Draft contracts "pending HIC approval"
- Do not collect deposits until registered

### If No Manufacturer Agreement Yet
- Use preliminary pricing from quotes
- Include "subject to final supplier confirmation" in proposals
- Focus on consultations and pipeline building

### If Leads Are Slow
- Increase partner outreach
- Consider paid Facebook/Instagram boost ($100-200)
- Host "Pool Planning Workshop" at local venue
