## Delaware Requirements

### Business License
- **Requirement:** Delaware business license from Division of Revenue
- **Cost:** $75-$300/year depending on structure
- **Website:** https://revenue.delaware.gov

### Contractor License
- **Requirement:** Delaware Contractor License (if performing construction)
- **Types:**
  - Residential (up to 3 stories)
  - Commercial
  - Specialty (pool/spa)
- **Exam:** Required for license
- **Insurance:** $300,000 minimum liability
- **Website:** https://dpr.delaware.gov

### Building Permits
- **Requirement:** Building permit from local jurisdiction
- **Documents needed:**
  - Site plan
  - Engineered drawings
  - Pool specifications
  - Electrical permit
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks typically

### Sussex/New Castle Counties
- Check local municipality for additional requirements
- Some require separate electrical/plumbing permits
- HOA approval may be needed

## Pennsylvania Requirements

### Contractor Registration
- **Requirement:** PA Contractor Registration (Attorney General)
- **Cost:** $65/year
- **Website:** https://www.attorneygeneral.gov

### Home Improvement Contractor
- **Requirement:** HIC license (for residential work)
- **Exam:** Pre-approval required, then exam
- **Insurance:** $50,000 minimum
- **Website:** https://www.dli.pa.gov

### Building Permits
- **Requirement:** Permit from local municipality
- **Documents:**
  - Construction/demolition permit
  - Zoning permit
  - Electrical permit
- **PA One Call:** Required before digging (811)

### Chester/Delaware Counties
- Local township building permits required
- Environmental permits may be needed (wetlands)
- Chesapeake Bay permit for some areas

## Insurance Coverage Needed

### General Liability
- **Minimum:** $1,000,000 per occurrence
- **Recommended:** $2,000,000 aggregate
- **Covers:** Property damage, bodily injury

### Workers' Compensation
- **Required** if employees
- **Cost:** ~2-3% of payroll
- **Covers:** Employee injuries

### Commercial Auto
- **Required** for company vehicles
- **Covers:** Vehicle damage, liability

### Professional Liability (E&O)
- **Recommended:** $500,000
- **Covers:** Design errors, omissions

### Umbrella Policy
- **Recommended:** $1,000,000+
- **Covers:** Excess liability

## Permit Checklist

### Pre-Construction
- [ ] Business license (DE/PA)
- [ ] Contractor license
- [ ] General liability insurance certificate
- [ ] Workers comp certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] EPA lead-safe certification (residential pre-1978)

### Pre-Dig
- [ ] PA One Call / DE Miss Utility (811)
- [ ] Soil analysis (if required)
- [ ] Engineering stamps (if required)

### Building Permit
- [ ] Site plan with setbacks
- [ ] Pool construction drawings
- [ ] Equipment specifications
- [ ] Electrical permit
- [ ] Plumbing permit (if applicable)

### Post-Construction
- [ ] Final inspection passed
- [ ] Electrical sign-off
- [ ] Pool bonding verification
- [ ] Warranty registration
