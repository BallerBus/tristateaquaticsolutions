## Our Process

### 1. Consultation & Design (Free)
- Site visit and assessment
- Pool size, shape, and feature preferences
- Equipment selection and budget range
- Design recommendations

### 2. Proposal & Agreement
- Detailed written proposal with specifications
- Timeline with key milestones
- Payment schedule and deposit requirements
- Contract review and signature

### 3. Permitting & Excavation
- Handle all municipal permits
- Complete site excavation
- Grading and site preparation

### 4. Installation
- Steel installation and framework
- Concrete gunite application
- Equipment installation (pump, filter, heater)
- Plumbing and electrical connections

### 5. Finishing & Handover
- Plaster and tile installation
- Startup and pool filling
- Owner orientation and training
- Warranty and maintenance guidance
