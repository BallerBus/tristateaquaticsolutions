## Dual-Brand Social Media Strategy

Tri-State Aquatic Solutions operates a **dual-brand approach** to maximize reach and cross-promote services:

| Brand | Focus | Role |
|-------|-------|------|
| **Pool Cleaning Dude** | Pool maintenance & service | Established brand with existing traction |
| **Tri-State Aquatic Solutions** | Pool installation (plunge, garden, cocktail pools) | Premium installation brand |

Both brands are operated by Dude Venture Services LLC and cross-promote each other.

## How the Two Brands Work Together

```
Customer needs maintenance/service
        |
        v
  Pool Cleaning Dude
  (existing accounts, established trust)
        |
        |--- Customer asks about new pool installation
        |
        v
  Refer to Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
  (premium installation brand)
        |
        |--- Installation complete
        |
        v
  Refer back to Pool Cleaning Dude
  (for ongoing maintenance and care)
```

### Key Rules
1. **Pool Cleaning Dude** handles all service/maintenance content and inquiries
2. **Tri-State Aquatic Solutions** handles all installation content and inquiries
3. Both brands mention each other naturally in relevant posts
4. Shared content (e.g., a completed project) can be posted on both accounts with different angles:
   - Tri-State: "Just completed this stunning plunge pool in Bryn Mawr"
   - Pool Cleaning Dude: "New pool? We handle the maintenance so you just enjoy the water"
