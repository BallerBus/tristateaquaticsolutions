### Monthly Keyword Discovery

1. **Google Search Console**
   - Export queries with impressions but low clicks
   - Identify new opportunities

2. **Competitor Analysis**
   - Run competitor keyword gap monthly
   - Add new relevant keywords

3. **Question Research**
   - Monitor "People Also Ask" boxes
   - Add new question keywords

4. **Seasonal Opportunities**
   - Track trending searches by season
   - Add seasonal keywords before peak

### Keyword Scoring System

| Factor | Weight | Score Range |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Search Volume | 30% | 1-10 |
| Business Relevance | 30% | 1-10 |
| Competition | 20% | 1-10 (lower is better) |
| Conversion Potential | 20% | 1-10 |

**Priority Score Formula:**
```
Priority = (Volume × 0.3) + (Relevance × 0.3) + ((11 - Competition) × 0.2) + (Conversion × 0.2)
```
