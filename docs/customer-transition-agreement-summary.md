# Customer Transition Agreement - Summary of Terms

**Document Type:** Summary for Discussion (Not a Legal Contract)
**Date Prepared:** February 4, 2026

---

## Parties

| Role | Company | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Referring Party** | Spa Hearth & Home | Maple Shade, NJ |
| **Receiving Party** | Tristate Aquatic Solutions | Hockessin, DE |

---

## Purpose

Randy (Spa Hearth & Home) is transferring service responsibility for Delaware and Philadelphia-area customers to Tristate Aquatic Solutions to ensure these customers receive quality local service from a trusted provider in their geographic area.

---

## Customer Categories & Payment Terms

### Outdoor Pool Service (Seasonal)
- **Season:** Memorial Day through Labor Day
- **Referral Fee:** 15% of seasonal service revenue
- **Payment Due:** Within 30 days of Labor Day
- **Duration:** Year 1 only

### Spa & Indoor Pool Service (Year-Round)
- **Referral Fee:** 15% of service revenue
- **Payment Schedule:** Quarterly (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- **Duration:** Year 1 only

### After Year 1
- No further referral payments required
- Customer relationships transfer fully to Tristate Aquatic Solutions

---

## Randy's Responsibilities (Spa Hearth & Home)

- [ ] Provide complete customer contact information for all transferred accounts
- [ ] Provide service history documentation for each account
- [ ] Make personal introductions to each customer (via email or phone call)
- [ ] Honor 3-year non-compete on all transferred customers
- [ ] Disclose any known issues, complaints, or difficult situations with accounts

---

## Tristate's Responsibilities (Tristate Aquatic Solutions)

- [ ] Maintain high service quality standards consistent with Randy's reputation
- [ ] Honor any existing service commitments through the current season
- [ ] Provide quarterly customer retention reports to Randy
- [ ] Treat all customers professionally, protecting Randy's reputation and relationships

---

## Retention Guarantee

If more than **25% of transferred customers** discontinue service within the **first 90 days** due to pre-existing issues (not attributable to Tristate's service), referral payments will be reduced proportionally.
