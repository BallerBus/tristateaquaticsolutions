## Brand Standards for Presentations

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deep Navy | #0E1F34 | Slide backgrounds, headers |
| Aqua Blue | #00A6B2 | Accents, CTAs, highlights |
| Warm Gold | #C9A962 | Premium touches, dividers |
| Soft Gray | #F5F7FA | Alternate backgrounds |
| Pure White | #FFFFFF | Text on dark, clean slides |

### Typography
| Usage | Font | Weight |
|-------|------|--------|
| Slide Titles | Montserrat | Bold |
| Subheadings | Montserrat | SemiBold |
| Body Text | Open Sans | Regular |
| Captions | Open Sans | Light |
| Data/Numbers | Open Sans | SemiBold |

### Slide Dimensions
- **Standard:** 16:9 (1920 x 1080px)
- **Print-friendly:** 4:3 option available
- **Mobile viewing:** Optimize text for 40pt minimum

## 5. Template Specifications

### Master Slide Elements

#### Title Slides
- Background: Full-bleed photo with navy gradient overlay (70% opacity)
- Logo: White version, top-left corner
- Title: Montserrat Bold 48pt, white
- Subtitle: Open Sans Regular 24pt, gold (#C9A962)

#### Content Slides
- Background: White or soft gray (#F5F7FA)
- Header bar: Navy (#0E1F34) with slide title
- Body text: Open Sans Regular 16-18pt
- Bullet points: Aqua (#00A6B2) dots
- Page number: Bottom right, navy, 10pt

#### Image Slides
- Full-bleed images with semi-transparent overlays
- Text boxes with navy or white backgrounds
- Minimum 60% image, 40% content ratio

#### Data Slides
- Charts/graphs: Navy, aqua, gold palette
- Tables: Navy headers, alternating row colors
- Icons: Line-style, aqua accent

### File Formats
- **Source:** Google Slides or PowerPoint (.pptx)
- **Distribution:** PDF (high-quality)
- **Images:** PNG/JPG, minimum 1920px wide

### Animation Guidelines
- Minimal animation - professional, not flashy
- Fade transitions only (0.3 seconds)
- Build slides for complex information
- No sound effects
