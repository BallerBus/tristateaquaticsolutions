### Design Guidelines
- Navy background with white/gold text for visibility
- Logo prominent at top
- Phone number large and readable from 30+ feet
- Consider 2-sided printing
- Weather-resistant materials

### Print Specifications
- **Size:** 18" x 24" (standard) or 24" x 36" (large)
- **Material:** 4mm Corrugated Plastic (Coroplast)
- **Printing:** Full color, 2-sided
- **Stakes:** Wire H-stakes (included)
- **Quantity:** 10-25 signs

### Placement Guidelines
- Obtain homeowner permission before placing
- Position for maximum street visibility
- Remove within 2 weeks of project completion
- Keep signs clean and upright
