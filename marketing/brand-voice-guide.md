## Writing Style Checklist

Before publishing any content, check:

- [ ] Does it sound like something Brandon would actually say out loud?
- [ ] Is there a clear benefit to the customer (not just features)?
- [ ] Are sentences short and scannable?
- [ ] Did we mention we're local/owner-operated?
- [ ] Is the CTA specific and easy to follow?
- [ ] No corporate fluff words?
- [ ] Would a pool owner actually care about this?
