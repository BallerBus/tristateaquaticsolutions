## 2. Sponsorship Tiers

### Bronze Tier: $250 - $500

**What You Get:**
- Logo on event materials/website
- Social media mention (1-2 posts)
- Company name listed in program
- Small banner display opportunity

**Best For:**
- Youth sports team jerseys
- Small community events
- Local 5K water stations
- Church/community organization events

**ROI Expectations:**
- Brand awareness in target neighborhoods
- 2-5 lead inquiries per sponsorship
- Cost per impression: $0.05-0.10

**Annual Budget Allocation:** 6-8 Bronze sponsorships = $1,500-4,000

---

### Silver Tier: $500 - $1,500

**What You Get:**
- Prominent logo placement on materials
- Multiple social media mentions
- Email newsletter inclusion (reach: 5,000-15,000)
- Table/booth at event
- Banner display opportunity
- Link on sponsor webpage for 1 year

**Best For:**
- Chamber of Commerce events
- Community festivals
- Charity 5K title sponsorships
- School athletic programs

**ROI Expectations:**
- 5-15 qualified lead inquiries
- Direct engagement with 100-500 attendees
- Cost per lead: $50-150

**Annual Budget Allocation:** 4-6 Silver sponsorships = $2,000-9,000

---

### Gold Tier: $1,500 - $5,000

**What You Get:**
- Premium logo placement (top tier)
- Featured sponsor recognition
- Speaking opportunity at event
- Prime booth location
- Multiple email/social mentions
- Press release inclusion
- VIP access/tickets
- Year-round website presence

**Best For:**
- Home & garden shows
- Major charity golf tournaments
- Chamber annual events
- Large community festivals

**ROI Expectations:**
- 15-50 qualified leads
- Direct customer conversations
- Media exposure/PR value
- Cost per lead: $30-100

**Annual Budget Allocation:** 2-4 Gold sponsorships = $3,000-20,000

---

### Platinum/Title Tier: $5,000+

**What You Get:**
- Exclusive category sponsorship (no competing pool companies)
- Event naming rights where applicable
- Premier logo placement on all materials
- Featured in all marketing/advertising
- Keynote or featured speaker opportunity
- Premium booth location + additional space
- Exclusive access to attendee list (where permitted)
- Year-round marketing partnership
- Custom activation opportunities

**Best For:**
- Major home shows (premiere pool sponsor)
- High-profile charity events
- Chamber signature events

**ROI Expectations:**
- 50+ qualified leads
- Significant brand positioning
- Media coverage
- Cost per lead: $50-100

**Annual Budget Allocation:** 1-2 Platinum sponsorships = $5,000-10,000

---

### Recommended Annual Sponsorship Budget

| Budget Level | Annual Investment | Expected Leads | ROI |
|--------------|-------------------|----------------|-----|
| **Starter** | $5,000 | 30-50 leads | 5-10x |
| **Growth** | $10,000 | 60-100 leads | 8-15x |

### C. Lead Capture Strategies

**Digital Lead Capture (Primary):**
1. iPad with lead capture form (JotForm, Typeform, or CRM-connected)
2. QR code to download Pool Cost Guide (captures email)
3. Text-to-win contest entry

**Contest/Raffle Entry (Secondary):**
- Entry form collects: Name, Email, Phone, Address, Pool Interest Level, Timeline
- Drawing for grand prize (e.g., $500 pool service credit)
- Follow-up: All entrants receive thank-you email + educational content

**Engagement Tactics:**
- "Pool Dreams" interactive display: "What's your dream backyard?" photo voting
- Water quality test strips giveaway (requires conversation)
- "Spin the wheel" for prizes (captures attention)
- Before/after guessing game (how long did this install take?)

**Lead Qualification Questions:**
1. Do you currently have a pool? (Yes/No)
2. Have you considered adding a pool? (Yes/No)
3. When are you thinking about installation? (This year / Next 2 years / Just exploring)
4. What's most important to you? (Speed / Quality / Price / Design)

### D. Brand Visibility Tactics

**At Events:**
- Wear branded shirts/polos (navy with aqua accents)
- Staff should be personable, knowledgeable, never pushy
- Position banners for maximum foot traffic visibility
- Use lighting if indoor event (portable spotlights on display)

**Pre-Event Marketing:**
- Share event details on social media (tag event organizers)
- Email existing customers about the event
- Mention sponsorship in any advertising

**During Event:**
- Take photos at booth (with permission)
- Post social media updates in real-time
- Get video testimonials from interested visitors
- Connect with other vendors for referral partnerships

**Post-Event:**
- Thank the organization publicly on social media
- Share event photos (tag attendees if appropriate)
- Send thank-you note to organizers
- Follow up with all leads within 48 hours

- Phone
- Address (city/zip)

- Timeline
- Property type (existing home / new construction)
- Preferred contact method
- Notes

## 7. Yard Sign & Local Visibility Program

### Yard Sign Program

**Sign Specifications:**
- Size: 18" x 24" (standard) or 24" x 36" (premium)
- Material: Corrugated plastic (4mm thickness)
- Stake: H-frame wire (15" or 18")
- Design: Navy background, Aqua accent, White text
- Content: Company name, Phone, Website, "Now Installing in [Neighborhood]"

**Cost Breakdown:**
| Quantity | Cost per Sign | Total | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|-------|
| 25 signs | $18-22 | $450-550 | Starter batch |
| 50 signs | $15-18 | $750-900 | Standard order |
| 100 signs | $12-15 | $1,200-1,500 | Volume discount |
| 200 signs | $10-12 | $2,000-2,400 | Full deployment |

**Placement Strategy:**
1. Every active job site (mandatory)
2. Completed projects (with customer permission, $50 credit offered)
3. Corner lots on main roads (highest visibility)
4. Near schools, parks, community centers (with permission)
5. Subdivision entrances (with HOA approval)

**Duration:**
- Active job sites: Duration of project + 30 days
- Completed projects: 60-90 days post-completion
- Offer annual discount for permanent placement

**ROI:**
- Cost per lead: $4-7
- Expected leads per sign: 3-5 over 30-60 days
- Conversion to sale: 15-20%
- Revenue per sign: $7,500-15,000 potential
