## 2. Blog-to-Social Workflow

### Step-by-Step Extraction Process

#### Step 1: Content Analysis (15 minutes)
Read through the blog post and identify:
- [ ] 3-5 key statistics or data points
- [ ] 5-7 quotable sentences
- [ ] Main argument/thesis
- [ ] Supporting points
- [ ] Visual opportunities (charts, comparisons)
- [ ] Questions the content answers

#### Step 2: Platform Mapping (10 minutes)
Assign extracted content to platforms:

| Content Element | Best Platform | Format |
|-----------------|---------------|--------|
| Statistics | Twitter, LinkedIn | Text post with graphic |
| Quotes | Instagram, Pinterest | Quote card |
| How-to lists | Facebook, LinkedIn | List post or carousel |
| Before/after | Instagram, Pinterest | Carousel or Reel |
| Questions | All platforms | Engagement post |
| Detailed breakdown | LinkedIn | Long-form post |

Transform each element for platform requirements:

## 3. Video Repurposing Guide

### Long-Form to Short-Form Clips

#### Video Clip Extraction Framework

For every 5+ minute video, extract:
| Clip Type | Duration | Quantity | Best Platforms |
|-----------|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Teaser clips** | 15-30 sec | 2-3 | Instagram Reels, TikTok |
| **Key moment clips** | 30-60 sec | 4-6 | All short-form |
| **Extended clips** | 1-2 min | 2-3 | YouTube Shorts, Facebook |
| **Full segments** | 2-4 min | 1-2 | YouTube, Facebook |

#### Identifying Clip-Worthy Moments

Look for:
- [ ] Strong statements or opinions
- [ ] Statistics or surprising facts
- [ ] Before/after reveals
- [ ] Emotional moments (customer reactions)
- [ ] Demonstration of expertise
- [ ] Behind-the-scenes glimpses
- [ ] Common question answers
- [ ] Tips or actionable advice

#### Clip Optimization for Each Platform

**Instagram Reels (15-90 seconds)**
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Text overlay for silent viewing
- Trending audio when appropriate
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Caption with CTA

**TikTok (15-60 seconds preferred)**
- Faster pace than Instagram
- Native TikTok trending sounds
- Authentic, less polished feel
- Trending hashtags
- Engagement hooks ("Save this!")

**YouTube Shorts (under 60 seconds)**
- Can be more educational
- Vertical format required
- Searchable titles matter
- Link to full video in comment

**Facebook Reels (15-90 seconds)**
- Similar to Instagram Reels
- Slightly older demographic
- Professional quality appreciated
- Link in comments

**Step 1: Audio Extraction**
- Export audio track from video editing software

- Normalize audio levels

**Step 2: Podcast Formatting**
- Add standard intro (15-30 sec)

- Insert mid-roll breaks if needed
- Export as MP3 (128-192 kbps)

**Step 3: Podcast Metadata**
```
Title: [Topic] - Tri-State Aquatic Solutions Podcast
Description: [Expanded description with timestamps]
Show Notes: [Key points, links, resources]
```

**Step 4: Distribution**
- Upload to podcast host (Anchor, Libsyn, etc.)
- Distribute to: Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts
- Embed on website
- Share clips on social

WEEKLY MAINTENANCE

**Question:** "Should I get a fiberglass or concrete inground pool?"

Great question - this is probably the most common decision point for homeowners planning a pool. I build both types in the Pennsylvania/Delaware area, so I'll share what I've learned.

For most residential pools, fiberglass delivers better overall value. But concrete is the right choice for specific situations.

- Faster installation is important
- You prefer lower maintenance
- Long-term value matters to you
