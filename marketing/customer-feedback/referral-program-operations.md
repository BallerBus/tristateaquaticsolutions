## 2. Reward Fulfillment Process

### Reward Tiers

| Tier | Project Value | Reward | Additional Bonus |
|------|--------------|--------|------------------|
| Bronze | Under $30,000 | $250 gift card | - |
| Silver | $30,000-$50,000 | $500 gift card | Referrer choice of local restaurant |
| Gold | $50,000-$75,000 | $750 gift card | + Pool supply package ($100 value) |
| Platinum | $75,000+ | $1,000 gift card | + VIP status for 1 year |

### VIP Referrer Benefits

**Platinum Status (3+ converted referrals OR 1 referral $75k+):**
- Priority scheduling for service calls
- 15% discount on all future services
- Invitation to annual VIP customer event
- Birthday/holiday gift
- First access to new services/products

### Reward Options

**Primary Reward: Gift Card Selection**
- Visa/Mastercard prepaid card
- Amazon
- Local restaurants (rotating selection)
- Home improvement stores (Home Depot, Lowe's)
- Grocery stores (Kroger, Meijer)

**Alternative Rewards (by request):**
- Credit toward future service
- Pool supplies/chemicals
- Outdoor furniture credit
- Charitable donation in their name

### Fulfillment Timeline

```
Day 0: Referred customer signs contract
Day 1-3: Verify referral in tracking system
Day 3-5: Order reward
Day 7-10: Mail reward with thank-you package
Day 14: Follow-up to confirm receipt
```

### Fulfillment Checklist

**When referral converts to sale:**

- [ ] Verify referring customer in system
- [ ] Calculate correct reward tier based on contract value
- [ ] Check if referrer has reward preference on file
- [ ] Order appropriate gift card
- [ ] Prepare thank-you card (hand-signed by owner)
- [ ] Assemble reward package:
  - [ ] Gift card
  - [ ] Thank-you card
  - [ ] Program brochure (encourage more referrals)
  - [ ] Branded item (optional)
- [ ] Ship via USPS Priority or hand-deliver if local
- [ ] Update tracking system with fulfillment date
- [ ] Send "reward on the way" email
- [ ] Follow up to confirm receipt

### Reward Packaging

**Standard Package Contents:**
1. Branded envelope or small box
2. Gift card in presentation holder
3. Hand-written thank-you card
4. Program brochure with tier levels
5. Referral cards (blank, for next referral)

**Premium Package (Gold/Platinum):**
- All standard items plus:
- Branded merchandise (hat, tumbler, etc.)
- Pool supply gift basket
- Handwritten note from owner

## 4. Program Rules and Terms

### Official Program Terms

```
TRI-STATE AQUATIC SOLUTIONS REFERRAL PROGRAM
TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

1. ELIGIBILITY
   - Open to all current and past customers of Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
   - Referring party must have completed a project with us
   - Employees and immediate family members of employees are not eligible
   - Referred party must be a new customer (no previous quotes or consultations)

2. HOW TO REFER
   - Submit referrals via online form, email, phone, or in-person
   - Referral must include: referrer name, referred person's name, contact information
   - Referral must be submitted BEFORE the referred party contacts us

3. QUALIFYING REFERRALS
   - Referred party must sign a contract for a new pool construction or major renovation
   - Project minimum value: $15,000
   - Contract must be fully executed with deposit paid
   - Referral credit given to first referrer if multiple referrals received

4. REWARD STRUCTURE
   - Under $30,000: $250 reward
   - $30,000-$50,000: $500 reward
   - $50,000-$75,000: $750 reward
   - $75,000+: $1,000 reward
   - Rewards based on final contract value before any discounts

5. REWARD DELIVERY
   - Rewards issued within 30 days of contract signing and deposit receipt
   - Delivered via mail to address on file
   - Gift card format unless alternative requested
   - Rewards are non-transferable and have no cash value

6. LIMITATIONS
   - No limit on number of referrals per person
   - Referral rewards cannot be combined with other promotions for referrer
   - Company reserves the right to verify all referrals
   - Company reserves the right to modify or cancel program at any time

7. TAX RESPONSIBILITY
   - Referral rewards may be considered taxable income
   - Recipients are responsible for any applicable taxes
   - 1099 forms issued for total annual rewards exceeding $600

8. DISQUALIFICATION
   - Fraudulent referrals result in immediate disqualification
   - Self-referrals are not permitted
   - Referrals from lead generation services do not qualify

9. PRIVACY
   - Referral information is used solely for program purposes
   - We will not sell or share referrer or referred party information

10. MODIFICATIONS
    - Program terms subject to change with 30 days notice
    - Current terms apply to all pending referrals

For questions about the referral program:
Email: referrals@tristateaquatic.com
Phone: [Phone Number]

By participating in the referral program, you agree to these terms and conditions.
```

### FAQ for Customers

**Q: How do I make a referral?**
A: You can submit a referral online at tristateaquatic.com/referral, call us at [phone], email referrals@tristateaquatic.com, or tell your friend to mention your name when they contact us.

**Q: When do I receive my reward?**
A: Rewards are sent within 30 days of your referral signing a contract and paying their deposit.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
A: No! Refer as many people as you'd like. There's no cap on referrals or rewards.

**Q: What if I refer someone who was already talking to you?**
A: The referral must be submitted before the referred person contacts us. If they've already received a quote, the referral doesn't qualify.

**Q: Can I choose my reward?**
A: Yes! We offer various gift card options. Let us know your preference when you submit the referral or when we contact you about your reward.

**Q: What if my referral doesn't end up getting a pool?**
A: While we're sorry it didn't work out, rewards are only issued when referrals result in a signed contract. We still appreciate you thinking of us!

**Q: Do I have to pay taxes on my reward?**
A: Referral rewards may be considered taxable income. If your total rewards exceed $600 in a calendar year, we'll issue a 1099 form. Consult your tax advisor for guidance.

### ROI Calculation

```
Referral Program ROI = (Revenue from Referrals - Program Costs) / Program Costs x 100

Program Costs Include:
- Rewards paid
- Administrative time
- Marketing materials
- Software/tools

Example:
- Revenue from referrals: $500,000
- Rewards paid: $7,500
- Other costs: $2,500
- Total costs: $10,000
- ROI = ($500,000 - $10,000) / $10,000 = 4,900%
```
