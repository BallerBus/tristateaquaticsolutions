## 5. NPS Survey Implementation

### What is NPS?

Net Promoter Score (NPS) measures customer loyalty with a single question:

**"On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend Tri-State Aquatic Solutions to a friend or colleague?"**

### Scoring Categories

- **Promoters (9-10):** Loyal enthusiasts who will refer others
- **Passives (7-8):** Satisfied but unenthusiastic customers
- **Detractors (0-6):** Unhappy customers who may damage brand

### NPS Calculation

```
NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors
```

Score ranges from -100 to +100

### Industry Benchmarks

- Home services average: 45
- Construction/renovation: 40
- Our target: 60+

### NPS Survey Timing

| Touchpoint | Timing | Method |
|------------|--------|--------|
| Post-consultation | 24 hours | Email |
| Post-installation | 14 days | Email |
| 6-month check-in | 6 months | Email |
| Annual survey | 12 months | Email + Phone |
| After service call | 48 hours | Email/SMS |

### Follow-Up Protocol

**Promoters (9-10):**
1. Thank them immediately
2. Request online review within 24 hours
3. Ask for referrals
4. Invite to be a testimonial/case study

**Passives (7-8):**
1. Thank them for feedback
2. Ask: "What would it take to make your experience a 10?"
3. Document improvement opportunities
4. Follow up on implemented changes

**Detractors (0-6):**
1. Alert management immediately
2. Personal call from owner within 24 hours
3. Document specific issues
4. Create action plan for resolution
5. Follow up after resolution
6. Re-survey after issues addressed

## Survey Distribution Best Practices

### Email Guidelines

- Send from a recognizable email address (feedback@tristateaquatic.com)
- Use customer's first name in subject line
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Send during business hours (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm optimal)
- Mobile-optimize all surveys
- Include estimated completion time

### Response Rate Optimization

1. **Keep it short** - Respect time commitments stated
2. **Explain the "why"** - Tell customers how feedback will be used
3. **Offer incentives** - When appropriate
4. **Send reminders** - One reminder 3-5 days after initial send
5. **Close the loop** - Share improvements made from feedback

### Data Management

- Store all responses in CRM
- Tag responses by project type, timeline, and score
- Review weekly for trends
- Share with team monthly
- Quarterly deep-dive analysis
