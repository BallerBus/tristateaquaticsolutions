# Testimonial Collection Guide - Tri-State Aquatic Solutions

## Overview

Customer testimonials are powerful marketing assets that build trust and credibility. This guide covers how to systematically collect written testimonials, video testimonials, and customer photos while ensuring proper permissions and legal compliance.

---

## 1. Video Testimonial Interview Guide

### Why Video Testimonials Matter

- 72% of customers trust a business more after watching testimonials
- Video testimonials convert 25% better than written
- They showcase real customers and real pools
- Highly shareable on social media

### Pre-Interview Preparation

**Identifying Candidates:**
- NPS score of 9-10
- Enthusiastic in written feedback
- Visually impressive pool/backyard
- Articulate and comfortable on camera
- Diverse representation (family types, pool styles, locations)

**Scheduling the Interview:**
- Contact customer 4-6 weeks after completion
- Offer morning or late afternoon (best lighting)
- Allow 60-90 minutes total
- Confirm weather will be cooperative

**Equipment Checklist:**
- [ ] Camera (smartphone in 4K is fine, or professional camera)
- [ ] Tripod or stabilizer
- [ ] Lavalier microphone (critical for audio quality)
- [ ] Backup audio recorder
- [ ] Reflector for fill lighting
- [ ] Release forms (2 copies)
- [ ] Question list
- [ ] Business cards

### Interview Questions

**Opening (Warm-up):**
1. "Tell us a little about yourself and your family."
2. "What made you decide to get a pool?"

**The Decision Process:**
3. "How did you find Tri-State Aquatic Solutions?"
4. "What made you choose us over other pool builders?"
5. "What were your initial concerns about getting a pool installed?"

**The Experience:**
6. "Walk us through your experience working with our team."
7. "How did [Project Manager Name] help during the process?"
8. "Was there anything that surprised you about the experience?"
9. "How was communication throughout the project?"

**The Results:**
10. "How does your finished pool compare to what you originally envisioned?"
11. "What's your favorite feature of your new pool?"
12. "How has having a pool changed your family's lifestyle?"
13. "Describe your first time using the pool with your family."

**The Recommendation:**
14. "What would you say to someone considering Tri-State Aquatic Solutions?"
15. "Would you work with us again for future projects?"

**Closing:**
16. "Is there anything else you'd like to share about your experience?"

### Interview Tips for the Interviewer

**DO:**
- Make the customer comfortable with small talk first
- Position them with the pool visible behind them
- Ask them to include the question in their answer
- Let them speak naturally - don't interrupt
- Capture B-roll of the pool, family enjoying it, details
- Get multiple takes if needed
- Thank them sincerely

**DON'T:**
- Script their answers
- Coach them on what to say
- Rush the interview
- Film in harsh midday sun
- Forget to check audio levels
- Miss the release form signature

### Technical Requirements

**Video:**
- Resolution: 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Orientation: Landscape (horizontal)
- Duration: Aim for 5-10 min raw, edit to 60-90 seconds

**Audio:**
- Use external microphone
- Test levels before starting
- Monitor for wind noise
- Record room tone for editing

**B-Roll to Capture:**
- Wide shot of entire backyard/pool
- Pool water details (sparkle, reflection)
- Family using the pool
- Special features (waterfall, lighting, fire pit)
- Before/after comparisons (if available)
- Tight shots of tile, coping, equipment

### Post-Production

**Editing Guidelines:**
- Keep final video 60-90 seconds
- Include customer name and location (with permission)
- Add subtle music (royalty-free)
- Include Tri-State Aquatic Solutions branding
- Add call-to-action at end
- Create versions for: website, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook

**File Organization:**
```
/testimonials/
  /video/
    /[customer-lastname]/
      /raw/
      /edited/
      /thumbnails/
      release-form.pdf
```

## 2. Written Testimonial Request

### Email Request Template

**Subject:** Would you share your story?

```
Hi [First Name],

Thank you again for trusting Tri-State Aquatic Solutions with your pool project. We hope your family is making wonderful memories!

We'd be honored if you'd share your experience in a brief testimonial. Your story could help other families who are considering a pool make their decision with confidence.

It's easy - just reply to this email with a few sentences about:
- Why you chose Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
- What you enjoyed about working with us
- How you feel about your finished pool

Here's an example of what others have shared:

"We interviewed four pool companies before choosing Tri-State Aquatic Solutions. [Owner Name] took the time to understand exactly what we wanted and delivered beyond our expectations. Our kids practically live in the pool now! - The Martinez Family, Cincinnati"

If you're willing, we'd also love to include a photo of your pool (or your family enjoying it). You can simply attach it to your reply.

Thank you for considering this request. Your support means the world to our team!

Warm regards,

[Name]
Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
```

### Follow-Up Template (7 days later)

**Subject:** Quick reminder - share your pool story?

```
Hi [First Name],

Just a friendly follow-up on my previous email. If you have a moment to share a few words about your experience with Tri-State Aquatic Solutions, we'd truly appreciate it.

Even just 2-3 sentences would be wonderful!

Reply directly to this email, and I'll take care of the rest.

Thank you!

[Name]
```

### Testimonial Questionnaire (For Those Who Need Prompts)

For customers who say "I don't know what to write," offer this questionnaire:

```
Please answer any or all of these questions:

1. What was your biggest concern before starting your pool project?

2. What made you choose Tri-State Aquatic Solutions?

3. How would you describe your experience working with our team?

4. What's your favorite thing about your new pool?

5. How has having a pool changed your daily life or family time?

6. What would you tell a friend who's thinking about getting a pool?

7. Would you choose Tri-State Aquatic Solutions again?
```

### Testimonial Formatting

**Best Structure:**
1. Challenge/desire (why they wanted a pool)
2. Solution (why they chose us)
3. Results (how it turned out)
4. Recommendation (what they'd tell others)

**Length Guidelines:**
- Ideal: 50-100 words
- Include: First name, last initial or full name, city
- Add: Photo if available

**Example Formatted Testimonial:**
```
"After years of dreaming about a backyard pool, we finally took the plunge with Tri-State Aquatic Solutions. From the first consultation, [Owner Name] made us feel confident we were making the right choice. The entire process was smoother than we expected, and the team kept us informed every step of the way. Now, our pool is the centerpiece of our backyard and the favorite gathering spot for family and friends. We couldn't be happier!"

- Jennifer M., Lexington, KY
```

## 4. Usage Permissions

### Permission Levels

**Level 1: Full Permission**
- All marketing uses
- Name and location included
- Family photos allowed
- Video testimonial included
- No restrictions

**Level 2: Limited Permission**
- Website and social media only
- First name and city only
- Pool photos only (no people)
- No video

**Level 3: Minimal Permission**
- Portfolio only
- No names
- Pool photos only
- No advertising use

### Content Usage Matrix

| Content Type | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 |
|--------------|---------|---------|---------|
| Website portfolio | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Social media | Yes | Yes | No |
| Paid ads | Yes | No | No |
| Print brochures | Yes | No | No |
| Video content | Yes | No | No |
| Press/media | Yes | No | No |
| Trade shows | Yes | Yes | No |
| Customer name | Full | First + City | No |

### Digital Asset Management

**File Naming Convention:**
```
[year]-[customer-lastname]-[content-type]-[permission-level].ext

Examples:
2026-johnson-pool-wide-L1.jpg
2026-martinez-testimonial-video-L1.mp4
2026-smith-backyard-aerial-L2.jpg
```

**Required Metadata:**
- Customer name
- Project date
- Permission level
- Expiration date (if applicable)
- Release form location

### Annual Permission Audit

Each January, review:
- [ ] All release forms on file
- [ ] Expired limited releases
- [ ] Customers who've requested removal
- [ ] New content needing releases
- [ ] Database accuracy

## 5. Incentive Guidelines

### What's Allowed

**For Testimonials:**
- Thank-you gift card ($25-50 value) after providing testimonial
- Feature on website/social media (recognition)
- Priority scheduling for future service
- Invitation to exclusive customer events
- Entry into prize drawings

**For Video Testimonials:**
- Thank-you gift card ($50-100 value)
- Professional photos of their pool (we're already there)
- Social media feature with tags
- Case study feature on website

### What's NOT Allowed

- **Never offer incentives for reviews on third-party platforms** (Google, Yelp, Facebook, Houzz)
- This violates FTC guidelines and platform terms
- Can result in penalties and removed reviews

### Disclosure Requirements

When using incentivized testimonials:
- Must disclose the customer received something of value
- Add notation: "This customer received a gift card for sharing their experience"
- FTC requires clear and conspicuous disclosure

### Incentive Program Structure

**"Pool Champions" Program:**

1. **Bronze Level** - Written testimonial
   - $25 gift card to local restaurant
   - Thank-you card from owner

2. **Silver Level** - Written testimonial + photos
   - $50 gift card
   - Feature in monthly newsletter
   - 10% off first maintenance service

3. **Gold Level** - Video testimonial
   - $100 gift card
   - Professional pool photos (digital delivery)
   - Feature across all marketing
   - VIP invitation to annual customer event

### Gift Card Guidelines

- Purchase from local businesses when possible
- Keep receipts for tax purposes
- Track all incentives given
- Send within 2 weeks of receiving testimonial
- Include handwritten thank-you note

## Testimonial Collection Workflow

### Identifying Candidates

**Automatic Triggers:**
- NPS score of 9-10
- 5-star post-installation survey
- Unprompted positive feedback
- Social media praise

**Manual Selection Criteria:**
- Visually impressive project
- Compelling story
- Articulate customer
- Diverse representation

### Outreach Sequence

**Week 4 Post-Completion:**
1. Review customer's survey responses
2. If NPS 9-10: Add to testimonial candidate list
3. Send written testimonial request

**Week 5:**
4. Follow up on written request

**Week 6:**
5. If strong written response: Invite for video testimonial
6. Schedule video shoot

**Week 8:**
7. Conduct video testimonial
8. Collect signed release
9. Deliver incentive
