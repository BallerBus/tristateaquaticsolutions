## SLIDE 4: WHY FIBERGLASS IS IDEAL FOR HOA COMMUNITIES

**The Smart Choice for Governed Properties**

### Installation Benefits:

| Factor | Fiberglass | Concrete/Gunite |
|--------|------------|-----------------|
| **Installation Time** | 2-4 weeks | 3-6 months |
| **Yard Disruption** | Minimal | Extensive |
| **Noise & Dust** | Limited | Significant |

- **Consistent Appearance** - Smooth gel coat finish doesn't degrade

- **HOA Compliance** - Easier to maintain community aesthetic standards
- **Fewer Complaints** - Quick installation = fewer neighbor disruptions

- Color options that complement community aesthetics

## SLIDE 5: OUR INSTALLATION PROCESS

**Minimal Disruption. Maximum Results.**

### Week 1-2: Pre-Installation
- Site survey and underground utility check
- HOA plan submission (we handle this)
- Permit acquisition
- Material ordering

### Week 2-3: Installation Week
- **Day 1**: Excavation (one day, typically)
- **Day 2**: Pool shell delivery and setting
- **Day 3-5**: Backfill and plumbing connections
- **Day 6-7**: Equipment installation

### Week 3-4: Completion
- Electrical hookup (licensed electrician)
- Decking installation
- Fill, balance, and startup
- Owner walkthrough and training

### Our Commitment to Your Community:
- Work hours: 8 AM - 5 PM (no early starts or late nights)
- Daily cleanup of work areas
- Street sweeping after deliveries
- Immediate repair of any common area damage
- Regular communication with HOA liaison

## SLIDE 10: QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Common HOA Questions**

**Q: How do you handle complaints from neighbors during installation?**
A: We provide 48-hour advance notice to adjacent homeowners, maintain strict work hours, and offer direct contact to our project manager for any concerns.

**Q: What if a pool doesn't meet our architectural standards after installation?**
A: We work with your ARC/ACC committee before installation to ensure full compliance. Every design is pre-approved before ground is broken.

**Q: Can you provide references from other HOA communities?**
A: Yes, we can connect you with board members from communities where we've completed multiple installations.

**Q: What happens if something goes wrong after installation?**
A: Our warranty covers structural, equipment, and labor issues. We respond within 24 hours to warranty claims and maintain a local service team.

**Q: Do you work with our existing approved contractor list requirements?**
A: Yes, we can complete any vendor qualification process your community requires and provide all necessary documentation.
