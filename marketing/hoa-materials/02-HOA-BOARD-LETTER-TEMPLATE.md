## LETTER 5: PARTNERSHIP PROPOSAL

**[Print on Company Letterhead]**

[Date]

[HOA Name]
Attention: [Board President Name]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]

RE: Proposed Partnership Agreement - Pool Installation Services

Dear [Board President Name],

Following our recent discussions, I'm pleased to submit this formal partnership proposal for the board's consideration.

**Proposed Partnership: Preferred Vendor Status**

**Tri-State Aquatic Solutions agrees to:**

1. **Maintain Approved Vendor Status**
   - Provide updated Certificate of Insurance annually
   - Notify board of any changes to licensing or insurance
   - Complete any vendor qualification requirements

2. **Community Pricing**
   - Offer [X]% discount on standard pricing for [Community Name] homeowners
   - No price increases for 12 months from agreement date
   - Provide free in-home consultations

3. **Pre-Approved Designs**
   - Submit 5 design templates for ARC pre-approval
   - All installations must use pre-approved designs or obtain individual ARC approval
   - Color and material options aligned with community guidelines

4. **Installation Standards**
   - Work hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday - Friday
   - 48-hour advance notice to adjacent homeowners
   - Daily cleanup of work areas
   - Street sweeping after equipment deliveries
   - Repair any damage to common areas

5. **Communication**
   - Designated HOA liaison (Brandon Calloway, Owner)
   - Monthly status report during active installations
   - 24-hour response to board inquiries

6. **Ongoing Support**
   - Priority warranty service for community homeowners
   - Annual pool safety information session for residents
   - Maintenance service availability at community rates

**[Community Name] HOA agrees to:**

1. List Tri-State Aquatic Solutions as a preferred pool contractor
2. Provide Tri-State Aquatic Solutions information to homeowners inquiring about pools
3. Expedite ARC review for pre-approved designs
4. Allow informational materials in community communications (newsletter, website)

**Term:** 12 months, renewable annually by mutual agreement

**This is not an exclusive arrangement.** Homeowners remain free to select any qualified contractor, and the HOA may approve other vendors.

I'm happy to discuss any modifications to this proposal. Please contact me at 610-870-3113 to schedule a call.

Respectfully submitted,

Brandon Calloway
