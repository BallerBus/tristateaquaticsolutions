## SECTION 2: POOL GUIDELINES COMPLIANCE

### How We Ensure HOA Compliance

**Before Any Work Begins:**

1. **Design Review** - We submit detailed plans to ARC/ACC for approval
2. **Setback Verification** - We confirm all setback requirements are met
3. **Material Selection** - Pool colors and deck materials selected from approved palettes
4. **Fence Requirements** - All safety fencing meets local code AND HOA standards
5. **Equipment Placement** - Pump/filter locations minimized for noise and visibility

**Documentation We Provide:**

- Scaled site plan showing pool placement
- Equipment location diagram
- Deck/patio material specifications
- Pool color and finish samples
- Fencing specifications and placement
- Landscaping plan (if applicable)

### Common HOA Requirements We Address

| Requirement | Our Approach |
|-------------|--------------|
| Setback from property line | Surveyed and verified before design |
| Setback from house | Included in site plan |
| Maximum pool size | Design within specified limits |
| Fence height/style | Match community standards |
| Equipment screening | Landscape screening or enclosure options |
| Deck material | Pavers, concrete, or stone per guidelines |
| Color restrictions | Selection from approved options |

### ARC/ACC Application Support

We can prepare the complete ARC application package on behalf of the homeowner, including:

- Completed application form
- Site survey with pool placement
- Manufacturer specifications
- Color/material samples
- Contractor credentials
- Insurance certificate

## SECTION 4: COMMON HOA CONCERNS ADDRESSED

### Concern: Construction Disruption to Neighbors

**Our Response:**

Fiberglass pool installation is significantly less disruptive than concrete/gunite:

| Issue | Fiberglass Pool | Concrete Pool |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| Excavation | 1 day | 1-2 days |
| Heavy equipment on-site | 1-2 days | 2-4 weeks |
| Total construction time | 2-4 weeks | 3-6 months |
| Noise duration | Limited | Extended |
| Dust/debris | Minimal | Significant |

**Additional Measures:**
- 48-hour advance notice to adjacent homeowners
- Designated parking for work vehicles (off-street when possible)
- Street sweeping after equipment deliveries
- Work hours limited to 8 AM - 5 PM (or per HOA requirements)
