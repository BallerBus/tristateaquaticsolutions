# Community Bulk Discount Structure
## Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
### HOA Partnership Pricing Program

---

## PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Tri-State Aquatic Solutions offers exclusive pricing benefits to homeowners in HOA communities that partner with us. This program rewards communities for bulk referrals while maintaining our commitment to premium quality installation.

**Program Goals:**
- Provide meaningful savings to community homeowners
- Streamline the approval and installation process
- Build long-term relationships with quality communities
- Ensure consistent aesthetics across the community

---

## MULTI-HOME DISCOUNT TIERS

### How It Works

Discounts are applied based on the number of pool contracts signed within a 12-month period from a single HOA community. Discounts apply to the base pool installation package.

### Tier Structure

| Tier | Contracts per Year | Discount | Per-Pool Savings* |
|------|-------------------|----------|-------------------|
| **Bronze** | 1-2 pools | 3% | $1,500 - $2,100 |
| **Silver** | 3-4 pools | 5% | $2,500 - $3,500 |
| **Gold** | 5-7 pools | 7% | $3,500 - $4,900 |
| **Platinum** | 8+ pools | 10% | $5,000 - $7,000 |

*Based on typical pool installation costs of $50,000 - $70,000

### Tier Qualification Details

**Bronze Tier (3% Discount)**
- Minimum 1 pool contract signed
- Standard community pricing
- All standard warranties included
- Priority scheduling during peak season

**Silver Tier (5% Discount)**
- 3+ pool contracts in 12-month period
- Extended equipment warranty (4 years vs. 3)
- Complimentary pool school session for new owners
- Dedicated community liaison

**Gold Tier (7% Discount)**
- 5+ pool contracts in 12-month period
- All Silver benefits, plus:
- Free pool cover with each installation ($1,500 value)
- Annual community pool safety seminar
- First access to seasonal maintenance slots

**Platinum Tier (10% Discount)**
- 8+ pool contracts in 12-month period
- All Gold benefits, plus:
- Free automation upgrade with each pool ($2,500 value)
- Exclusive 24-month labor warranty
- VIP emergency response (same-day service)
- Community featured on our website/marketing (with permission)

## HOA BOARD MEMBER REFERRAL BONUSES

### Individual Referral Program

Board members who actively support the partnership receive personal benefits:

| Referral Milestone | Bonus |
|--------------------|-------|
| First referral that closes | $250 gift card |
| 3 referrals | $500 gift card + dinner for two |
| 5 referrals | $1,000 toward their own pool project |
| 10 referrals | Free pool opening/closing for life |

### How Referrals Are Tracked

1. Board member provides homeowner name/contact to us directly, OR
2. Homeowner mentions board member's name when inquiring
3. Contract must be signed within 90 days of referral
4. Bonus paid within 30 days of project completion

### Important Notes

- Bonuses available to any board member (not just president)
- Property manager referrals eligible at same rates
- Multiple board members can share referral credit for same homeowner (split bonus)
- Referral tracking available upon request

## PROGRAM TERMS & CONDITIONS

### Discount Eligibility

- Discounts apply to base pool installation package
- Add-ons and upgrades discounted at same tier rate
- Discounts cannot be combined with other promotions
- Tier status calculated on rolling 12-month basis

### Contract Requirements

- All contracts must be signed with Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
- Standard payment terms apply (1/3 deposit, per PA law)
- Discounts reflected in contract pricing (not rebated after)

### Tier Tracking

- New communities start at Bronze tier
- Tier upgrades apply to next contract after threshold is met
- We provide quarterly updates on community tier status
- Tier status maintained for 12 months from last completed project

### Program Modifications

- Pricing subject to change with 60-day notice
- Existing contracts honored at quoted prices
- Program terms may be updated annually
- HOA will be notified of any changes
