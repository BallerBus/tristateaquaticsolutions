**Q9: How does a saltwater pool work?**

Response:
```
[Name], saltwater pools have become our most popular request—about 80% of our installations now include saltwater systems.

Here's the simple version: A saltwater generator uses electrolysis to convert dissolved salt into chlorine. You still have chlorine sanitizing your water, but it's produced continuously at low, consistent levels rather than added manually in concentrated doses.

The benefits:
- Softer, silkier-feeling water
- No harsh chlorine smell
- Less eye and skin irritation
- Lower annual chemical costs
- More consistent water quality

We can install saltwater systems on new pools or convert existing chlorine pools. Happy to explain more.
```
