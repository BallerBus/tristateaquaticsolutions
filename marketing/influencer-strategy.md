# Tri-State Aquatic Solutions: Local Influencer & Content Creator Outreach Strategy

**Target Markets:** Main Line PA | Delaware | Greater Philadelphia Region
**Focus:** Home/Lifestyle Influencers, Interior Designers, Local Personalities

## 1. Influencer Identification Framework

### Types of Influencers to Target

| Category | Description | Why They Matter |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Home & Garden** | Backyard transformations, outdoor living | Direct audience alignment |
| **Interior Designers** | Design professionals with social presence | Upscale client referrals |
| **Lifestyle Creators** | Local living, family content | Community trust & reach |
| **Real Estate** | Agents showcasing luxury homes | High-value homeowner access |
| **Mom/Family** | Parenting & family lifestyle | Family pool market |
| **DIY/Home Improvement** | Renovation & home projects | Engaged homeowner audience |
| **Local Personalities** | News anchors, radio hosts, community figures | Broad local credibility |

### Platform Priorities

1. **Instagram** (Primary)
   - Best for visual before/after content
   - Strong local discovery via location tags
   - Stories and Reels for engagement
   - Target: 70% of outreach efforts

2. **TikTok** (Secondary)
   - Viral potential for transformation content
   - Younger homeowner demographic (30-45)
   - Behind-the-scenes appeal
   - Target: 20% of outreach efforts

3. **YouTube** (Tertiary)
   - Long-form documentation
   - Evergreen search visibility
   - Tutorial and educational content
   - Target: 10% of outreach efforts

### Follower Count Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Strategy | Expected Value |
|------|-----------|----------|----------------|
| **Nano** | 1K - 10K | High volume outreach, product/service trade | Authentic local engagement, 5-8% engagement rate |
| **Micro** | 10K - 50K | Collaborative projects, modest compensation | Targeted reach, 3-5% engagement rate |
| **Mid-Tier** | 50K - 100K | Paid partnerships, ambassador programs | Significant exposure, 2-4% engagement rate |
| **Macro** | 100K+ | Strategic partnerships, full productions | Brand awareness, 1-3% engagement rate |

**Primary Focus:** Nano and Micro influencers (80% of partnerships)

### Engagement Rate Benchmarks

- **Minimum Acceptable:** 2% engagement rate
- **Good:** 3-5% engagement rate
- **Excellent:** 5%+ engagement rate

**Calculation:** (Likes + Comments) / Followers x 100

### Local Relevance Criteria

**Must Have:**
- [ ] Located in or frequently posts about: Main Line PA, Delaware, Chester County, Delaware County, Montgomery County
- [ ] Audience primarily in target geographic area (check insights if possible)
- [ ] Content relevant to homeownership, lifestyle, or family

**Nice to Have:**
- [ ] Previously worked with local home service businesses
- [ ] Homeowner themselves
- [ ] Active community involvement
- [ ] Consistent posting schedule (3+ posts/week)

## 3. Collaboration Ideas

**Description:** Document the final reveal moment when homeowners see their completed pool for the first time.

**Content Format:**
- 60-90 second Reel/TikTok
- Emotional reaction capture
- Before shot transition to reveal

**Requirements:**
- Homeowner consent
- Morning/golden hour filming
- Multiple camera angles

**Best For:** Micro to mid-tier lifestyle influencers

---

### 2. Design Process Documentation
**Description:** Take followers through the consultation and design phase, showing how a backyard vision becomes a plan.

**Content Format:**
- 3-5 part story series
- Design meeting footage
- 3D rendering reveals
- Carousel post with design evolution

**Best For:** Interior designers, home improvement creators

---

### 3. Before/After Time-Lapse
**Description:** Capture the entire installation process condensed into a satisfying transformation video.

**Content Format:**
- 15-30 second time-lapse
- Drone footage if possible
- Side-by-side comparison

**Technical Needs:**
- GoPro or phone mount for consistent angle
- 4-8 week filming commitment
- Weekly check-in captures

**Best For:** DIY/home improvement creators, TikTok-focused creators

## 4. Compensation Framework

### What to Offer Influencers

| Tier | Primary Offer | Additional Perks |
|------|---------------|------------------|
| **Nano (1K-10K)** | Service trade, 10-15% project discount | Free design consultation, branded swag |
| **Micro (10K-50K)** | $200-500 per post + 10% discount | Content usage license, referral commission |
| **Mid-Tier (50K-100K)** | $500-1,500 per campaign | Full project documentation access, exclusive events |
| **Macro (100K+)** | $1,500-5,000+ negotiated | Custom packages, long-term contracts |

### Discount Structures

**One-Time Collaboration:**
- 10% discount on project total (up to $5,000 value)

**Ambassador Program:**
- 15% ongoing discount on any services
- Stackable with seasonal promotions (max 25%)

**Content Trade (for personal projects):**
- 20-30% discount in exchange for agreed content deliverables
- Must meet minimum content requirements

### Payment Guidelines

**Timing:**
- 50% upon content agreement signing
- 50% upon content delivery and approval

**Payment Methods:**
- Direct deposit/ACH
- PayPal
- Check (add 5 business days)

**Invoicing:**
- Influencers to provide W-9 for payments over $600
- Net 15 payment terms

### Content Ownership Terms

**Tri-State Aquatic Solutions Receives:**
- Perpetual license to use content on owned channels
- Rights to repurpose for advertising (with creator credit)
- Ability to share on website, social, and print materials

**Creator Retains:**
- Full ownership of original content
- Right to use in portfolio
- Right to repurpose for future content

**Exclusivity:**
- 90-day exclusivity for pool/outdoor living brands
- Non-exclusive with general lifestyle content

## Appendix: Tracking & Measurement

### KPIs to Track

| Metric | Target | Tracking Method |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Reach | 50K+ per campaign | Platform insights |
| Engagement Rate | 3%+ | (Likes + Comments) / Reach |
| Website Traffic | 500+ clicks/campaign | UTM tracking |
| Lead Generation | 5+ qualified leads/campaign | Form tracking |
| Follower Growth | 100+ new followers/campaign | Pre/post comparison |

### Campaign Tracking Spreadsheet Headers

- Influencer Name
- Platform
- Follower Count
- Engagement Rate
- Outreach Date
- Response Date
- Collaboration Type
- Compensation
- Content Deliverables
- Post Dates
- Reach Achieved
- Engagement Achieved
- Leads Generated
- Cost Per Lead
- Notes

### ROI Calculation

```
Campaign ROI = (Revenue from Attributed Leads - Total Campaign Cost) / Total Campaign Cost x 100
```

**Attribution Window:** 90 days from content posting
