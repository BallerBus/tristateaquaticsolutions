## Section 5: FAQ for Realtors

### About the Partnership

**Q: Do I need to sign a contract?**
A: No formal contract required. Submit referrals as they come. We track everything and pay you promptly.

**Q: Can I partner with other pool companies too?**
A: Absolutely. We don't require exclusivity. But we think you'll find our service and reliability make us your preferred choice.

**Q: What if my client doesn't move forward?**
A: No problem. You only earn commission on completed projects. No risk to you.

**Q: How do I track my referrals?**
A: We provide email confirmations at each stage: referral received, consultation scheduled, contract signed, project completed, payment issued.

**Q: Can my brokerage receive the commission instead?**
A: Yes. Let us know your preference and we'll issue payment accordingly.

### About Pool Installation

**Q: How long does installation really take?**
A: Fiberglass pools: 2-4 weeks of actual construction. Total timeline including design and permits: 8-12 weeks.

**Q: What's the price range?**
A: Small pools (12x24): $40,000-$55,000. Medium (16x32): $55,000-$75,000. Large/custom: $75,000-$100,000+.

**Q: Do you finance?**
A: Yes. We partner with Lyon Financial and HFS Financial. Terms up to 30 years, rates from 7.5%.

**Q: What warranty do you provide?**
A: Lifetime structural warranty on pool shell. 3-year equipment warranty. 2-year labor warranty.

**Q: Are you licensed and insured?**
A: Yes. Fully licensed in PA and DE. $2M liability coverage. Workers' comp for all employees.

### About Home Value

**Q: Will any pool increase home value?**
A: Quality pools from reputable installers add value. DIY or poorly installed pools can actually decrease value. That's why the installer matters.

**Q: What if the yard is small?**
A: Plunge pools and cocktail pools are perfect for smaller yards. Still add $30,000-$50,000 in value.

**Q: Should my seller install a pool before listing?**
A: Depends on the market and timeline. We can provide a "Pool Potential Assessment" to help you advise them.
