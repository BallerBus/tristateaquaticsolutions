## 1. Referral Program Structure

### Bonus Tier System

| Tier | Pool Type | Referral Bonus | Conditions |
|------|-----------|----------------|------------|
| **Standard** | Fiberglass Pool ($50K-$70K) | **$500 cash** | Paid upon project completion |
| **Premium** | Fiberglass Pool ($70K-$100K) | **$750 cash** | Paid upon project completion |
| **Luxury** | Pool + Hardscape ($100K+) | **$1,000 cash** | Paid upon project completion |
| **Plunge Pool** | Plunge Pool Installation | **$500 cash** | Paid upon project completion |

### Bonus Accelerators

| Accelerator | Additional Bonus | Details |
|-------------|------------------|---------|
| **Speed Bonus** | +$100 | Referral signs contract within 30 days of consultation |
| **Multi-Referral Bonus** | +$250 | 3+ referrals in a calendar year |
| **Ambassador Status** | +$250 per referral | After 3 successful referrals (see Section 8) |

### Eligibility Rules

**Who Can Refer:**
- Past customers with completed pool installations
- Current customers with signed contracts
- Professional partners (landscapers, realtors, builders) - see separate Partner Program
- Community members who receive a referral code at events

**Who Can Be Referred:**
- Homeowners in our service area (Main Line PA, Chester County, Northern Delaware)
- Must be a NEW customer (no previous quotes or consultations within 12 months)
- Property must meet minimum requirements for pool installation
- Must complete a full pool installation (not service-only)

**Exclusions:**
- Self-referrals
- Referrals from employees or immediate family of employees
- Previously quoted customers (within 12 months)
- Commercial properties (separate program available)

### Payment Process

**Timeline:**
1. Referral submits inquiry mentioning referrer's name OR uses referral code
2. Referral completes design consultation
3. Referral signs contract and pays deposit
4. Pool installation completed and final payment received
5. Referral bonus processed within **14 business days** of project completion
6. Referrer receives payment via check or ACH (their choice)

**Payment Methods:**
- **Check** - Mailed to referrer's address on file
- **ACH Direct Deposit** - Deposited within 3 business days of processing
- **Gift Card Option** - 10% bonus if taken as Amazon/Visa gift card ($550 instead of $500)

### Legal Considerations

**Tax Requirements:**
- All referral payments over $600/year will be reported via 1099-MISC
- Referrers will be asked to provide W-9 before second referral payment
- Referrer is responsible for reporting income and paying applicable taxes

**Terms and Conditions Summary:**
- Program terms may change with 30 days notice
- Referral bonus is forfeited if referrer requests refund on their own project
- One referral bonus per household referred
- Referral credit is assigned to the first referrer if multiple referrals occur
- Tri-State Aquatic Solutions reserves the right to modify or cancel program at any time

### Terms and Conditions (Full Version)

**TRI-STATE AQUATIC SOLUTIONS REFERRAL PROGRAM TERMS AND CONDITIONS**

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026

1. **Eligibility.** The Pool Party Rewards referral program ("Program") is open to customers of Tri-State Aquatic Solutions ("Company") who have completed a pool installation or have a signed contract for pool installation. Professional partners are eligible under separate Partner Program terms.

2. **Referral Requirements.** A valid referral must be a new customer who has not received a quote from the Company within the preceding 12 months. The referred party must complete a full pool installation with the Company. The referral must identify the referrer by name or unique referral code at the time of initial consultation.

3. **Referral Bonus Amounts.** Bonus amounts are determined by the total contract value of the referred customer's project: $500 for projects valued at $50,000-$69,999; $750 for projects valued at $70,000-$99,999; $1,000 for projects valued at $100,000 or more.

5. **Payment Methods.** Referrers may elect to receive payment via check or ACH direct deposit. Referrers selecting gift card payment will receive a 10% bonus on the referral amount.

6. **Tax Obligations.** Referral payments constitute taxable income. The Company will issue IRS Form 1099-MISC for cumulative annual referral payments of $600 or more. Referrers are responsible for providing accurate tax information (W-9) upon request.

7. **Multiple Referrals.** If more than one referrer claims credit for the same referred customer, credit will be assigned to the first referrer to submit the referral or whose name/code was provided at initial contact.

8. **Forfeiture.** Referral bonuses are forfeited if the referrer cancels their own project or requests a refund prior to the referred customer's project completion.

9. **Program Modifications.** The Company reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate the Program at any time with 30 days written notice to active participants.

10. **Limitation of Liability.** The Company's total liability under this Program is limited to the referral bonus amount. This Program creates no employment or agency relationship between the referrer and the Company.

11. **Governing Law.** This Program is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

For questions about the Program, contact us at referrals@tristateaquatic.com or (302) XXX-XXXX.
