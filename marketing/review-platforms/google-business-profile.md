## 1. Complete Profile Setup

### Business Information

**Business Name:**
- Use exact legal business name: "Tri-State Aquatic Solutions"
- Do NOT add keywords, locations, or taglines to the name
- Violating naming guidelines can result in suspension

**Address Configuration:**

For service-area businesses (SAB) like pool installation:
- Hide street address from public listing
- Configure service areas instead of storefront location
- This allows appearing in searches across your entire service region

Set service areas by cities/townships:

## 6. Q&A Management

### Pre-Populated Q&A Strategy

Seed your profile with common questions and answers. Post from your business account.

### 15 Essential Q&A Entries

**Cost Questions:**

**Q1: How much does an inground pool cost in the Main Line/Delaware area?**
```
A: Pool costs in our service area typically range from $65,000 to $150,000+ depending on pool type, size, and features. Fiberglass pools start around $65,000-$85,000 for smaller designs and $85,000-$135,000 for larger pools with premium features. Custom concrete pools range from $70,000 for standard designs to $150,000+ for estate-level projects. We provide detailed, transparent quotes during your free design consultation.
```

**Q2: Do you offer financing for pool installation?**
```
A: Yes, we work with several financing partners to make pool ownership accessible. Options include personal loans, home equity loans, and manufacturer financing programs with terms from 5-15 years. Many homeowners find monthly payments comparable to a car payment while adding significant value to their home. We'll discuss financing options during your consultation.
```

**Q3: Does a pool add value to my home?**
```
A: In the Main Line and Northern Delaware markets, a well-designed pool typically adds 5-15% to your home's value, with higher returns in luxury neighborhoods. Beyond resale value, a pool dramatically increases your enjoyment of your home and outdoor living space. We design pools that complement your home's architecture to maximize both lifestyle and investment value.
```

**Timeline Questions:**

**Q4: How long does pool installation take?**
```
A: Installation timelines vary by pool type:
- Fiberglass pools: 2-4 weeks from excavation to first swim
- Vinyl liner pools: 4-6 weeks
- Concrete/gunite pools: 3-5 months

Fiberglass pools are significantly faster because the shell is pre-manufactured. Concrete pools require on-site construction and curing. We provide a detailed timeline specific to your project during consultation.
```

**Q5: When is the best time to install a pool?**
```
A: The ideal time to START planning is fall or winter for spring/summer completion. Pool installation can happen year-round in our area, though spring is most popular. If you want to swim by Memorial Day, we recommend beginning your design process by January-February. Off-season installation often offers more flexible scheduling.
```

**Q6: Do you handle permits and approvals?**
```
A: Absolutely. We handle all permitting and approvals including building permits, zoning applications, electrical permits, and fence inspections. We have extensive experience with Lower Merion, Radnor, Haverford, Tredyffrin, and Delaware townships. Our familiarity with local codes helps avoid delays and ensures your pool meets all requirements.
```

**Pool Type Questions:**

**Q7: Is fiberglass or concrete better for Pennsylvania weather?**
```
A: Fiberglass pools are excellent for Pennsylvania's freeze-thaw climate because the shell flexes with ground movement rather than cracking. They also require less maintenance and fewer chemicals. Concrete pools offer unlimited design flexibility for complex shapes and very large pools. We help you choose the right type based on your property, design goals, and preferences.
```

**Q8: What is a plunge pool?**
```
A: Plunge pools are compact pools (10-16 feet) designed for smaller spaces or homeowners who want pool benefits without a large footprint. They're perfect for cooling off, entertaining, or wellness purposes like cold plunge therapy. Plunge pools can be heated for year-round enjoyment and installed in as little as 2 weeks.
```

**Q9: What's the difference between saltwater and chlorine pools?**
```
A: Saltwater pools use a generator to convert salt into chlorine, producing gentler, more consistent sanitization. Benefits include softer-feeling water, less chlorine smell, reduced eye irritation, and lower chemical costs. The salt level is about 1/10th of ocean water. Most of our clients prefer saltwater for its comfort and lower maintenance. We can install on new pools or convert existing ones.
```

**Service Area Questions:**

**Q10: What areas do you serve?**
```
A: We serve the Main Line Philadelphia area and Northern Delaware including:

Pennsylvania: Gladwyne, Villanova, Bryn Mawr, Haverford, Radnor, Wayne, Malvern, Paoli, West Chester, and surrounding Chester, Montgomery, and Delaware County communities.

Delaware: Hockessin, Greenville, Wilmington, Centreville, and New Castle County.

If you're unsure about your area, give us a call.
```

**Company Questions:**

**Q11: Are you licensed and insured?**
```
A: Yes, Tri-State Aquatic Solutions is fully licensed in both Pennsylvania and Delaware with comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide certificates of insurance upon request. We maintain industry association memberships and stay current on installation techniques and safety standards.
```

**Q12: Do you offer pool maintenance after installation?**
```
A: Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages including weekly or bi-weekly cleaning, chemical balancing, equipment inspection, and seasonal opening/closing. Many clients appreciate having one trusted company handle everything from installation through ongoing care. We also service pools we didn't install.
```

**Feature Questions:**

**Q13: Can you add a spa to my pool design?**
```
A: Absolutely. Integrated pool and spa combinations are one of our most popular designs. Options include spillover spas, raised spas with waterfalls, and separate spas with independent temperature control. Spas can be heated year-round, extending your outdoor enjoyment through Pennsylvania winters.
```

**Q14: What safety features do you recommend for families with children?**
```
A: We recommend multiple layers of protection:
- Automatic pool covers (ASTM-certified)
- Self-closing, self-latching fence gates
- Pool alarms
- Slip-resistant decking
- Gradual entry steps with handrails

Automatic covers are the gold standard - they prevent unsupervised access while reducing heating costs. We discuss all safety options during consultations.
```

**Getting Started:**

**Q15: How do I get started with a pool project?**
```
A: Getting started is easy:
1. Request a free consultation through our website or call us
2. We'll schedule an on-site visit to assess your property and discuss your vision
3. You'll receive a custom design with 3D renderings and detailed proposal
4. Once approved, we handle permits and schedule installation
5. Enjoy your new backyard retreat!

There's no obligation for the initial consultation. Contact us today!
```

## 7. Review Management

### Review Generation Strategy

Unlike Yelp, Google allows and encourages review requests.

**Create Direct Review Link:**

1. Go to Google Maps
2. Search for your business
3. Click "Write a review"
4. Copy the URL from browser

Or use Place ID method:
1. Get your Place ID from Google
2. Create link: `https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID`

**Shortened Link:**
Create a branded short link: `bit.ly/TSAreview`

### Review Request Timing

| Touchpoint | Timing | Method |
|------------|--------|--------|
| Final walkthrough | Day 0 | Verbal + card |
| Email 1 | Day 1 | Thank you + ask |
| SMS 1 | Day 1-2 | Quick ask |
| Email 2 | Day 3-4 | Gentle reminder |
| Email 3 | Day 7 | Story angle |
| Phone check-in | Day 7-14 | During call |

### Review Response Templates

**5-Star Response:**
```
[Name], thank you so much for this wonderful review! It was truly a pleasure working with your family on your [pool type] pool. We loved seeing your vision come to life, and we're thrilled you're enjoying your new backyard retreat.

If you ever need anything, we're always here. Welcome to the Tri-State family!

- [Owner Name], Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
```

**4-Star Response:**
```
Thank you for the positive feedback, [Name]! We're glad you're happy with your pool. If there's anything we could have done to make your experience even better, we'd love to hear about it.

Enjoy your beautiful backyard!

- [Owner Name]
```

**Negative Review Response:**
```
[Name], thank you for sharing your feedback. We're sorry to hear that your experience didn't meet your expectations. This is not the level of service we strive to provide.

I'd like to personally look into what happened and find a resolution. Please contact me directly at [phone] so we can discuss this privately.

Your satisfaction is important to us.

- [Owner Name], Owner, Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
```

### Review Response Best Practices

**Always:**
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Use the reviewer's name
- Be genuine and personal
- Reference specific details from their review
- Include relevant keywords naturally
- Sign with a real name

**Never:**
- Be defensive or argumentative
- Use generic copy-paste responses
- Offer compensation publicly
- Ask for review removal publicly
- Ignore negative reviews

## 8. Advanced Optimization

### Google Local Services Ads

Consider enrolling in Local Services Ads for the "Google Guaranteed" badge:

**Benefits:**
- Appears at top of search results
- "Google Guaranteed" trust badge
- Pay per lead, not per click
- Background check verification builds trust

**Requirements:**
- Business license verification
- Insurance verification
- Background checks on owner/employees
- Customer reviews and rating maintained

### Google Posts Performance

Track post performance through Insights:

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| Views | Post visibility |
| Clicks | Engagement level |
| Calls | Direct response |
| Direction requests | Intent to visit |

### Competitive Analysis

Monitor competitor GBP profiles for:
- What categories they're using
- Photo quantity and quality
- Review velocity and ratings
- Post frequency
- Q&A presence

### Reputation Management

**Set Up Alerts:**
- Google Alerts for business name
- Mention monitoring for brand
- Weekly review platform checks

**Handle Fake Reviews:**
1. Flag the review through GBP
2. Document why it's fake
3. Don't mention suspicions publicly
4. Follow up with Google if not removed
