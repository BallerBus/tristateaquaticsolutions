# Black Friday Promotion
## Off-Season Booking Event

**Campaign Period:** Wednesday, November 25 - Monday, November 30, 2026 (Thanksgiving Week)
**Campaign Theme:** "Black Friday Pool Deals"
**Primary Goal:** Maximize off-season deposits with biggest discounts of the year
**Format:** 5-day promotional event with tiered offers

---

## Executive Summary

Black Friday is universally recognized as the biggest savings event of the year. By participating with significant pool discounts, we tap into the cultural expectation of exceptional deals while capturing serious buyers during their planning window.

This promotion overlaps with our fall campaign deadline, creating compounding urgency.

---

## Campaign Strategy

### Core Positioning
Black Friday isn't just for TVs and gadgets. This is the biggest pool discount we offer all year - a rare opportunity to save on something that actually changes your life.

### Key Elements
1. **Biggest discount of the year** ($8,000-$10,000)
2. **Black Friday cultural recognition**
3. **Off-season + deadline synergy**
4. **Deposit-focused** (low barrier to lock in)
5. **Tiered offers** (standard vs. premium)

### Unique Angle
"While everyone else is buying stuff that ends up in a closet, you're investing in a lifetime of family memories."
