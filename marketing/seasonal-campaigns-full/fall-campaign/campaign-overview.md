# Fall Campaign: "Plan Now for Next Year"
## Complete Campaign Overview

**Campaign Period:** September 1 - November 30, 2026
**Campaign Theme:** "Maximum Savings, Minimum Stress"
**Primary Goal:** Secure off-season deposits and build spring 2027 pipeline
**Secondary Goal:** Complete remaining fall installations and drive pool closing services

---

## Executive Summary

The Fall Campaign is our most value-focused campaign of the year. We offer the largest discounts ($7,500-$10,000) to reward homeowners who plan ahead during our slower season.

This campaign targets:
1. **Strategic planners** who want to save money and avoid spring rush
2. **Year-round dreamers** who've been thinking about pools all year
3. **Deal seekers** attracted by significant off-season savings
4. **Smart homeowners** who understand the value of planning ahead

---

## Campaign Strategy

### Core Positioning
Position off-season planning as the sophisticated, financially smart choice. These aren't "leftovers" - these are premium discounts for premium planners.

### Key Differentiators to Emphasize
1. **Largest savings of the year** ($7,500-$10,000 off)
2. **Design without rush** (full attention, thoughtful process)
3. **2027 spring priority** (first to swim while others wait)
4. **Price protection** (lock in 2026 rates before increases)
5. **No competition** (choose your preferred installation window)

### Emotional Drivers
- **Smart/Strategic:** Making a financially savvy decision
- **Relaxed:** No pressure, no rush, no stress
- **Ahead of the game:** While others wait, you're already planned
- **Satisfied:** Finally taking action after thinking about it all year
