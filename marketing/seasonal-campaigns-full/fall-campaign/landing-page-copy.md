## SECTION 8: FAQ

### Q: Why is fall pricing so much lower?
A: Simple economics. Spring is our busiest season - high demand means we charge market rates. Fall has lower demand, so we pass significant savings to customers who plan ahead. It's our way of filling our spring calendar early.

### Q: What if I'm not ready to decide yet?
A: That's actually ideal for fall planning. You don't need to rush. Schedule a no-obligation consultation, explore your options, and take your time deciding. The deposit isn't due until you're ready (but before November 30 to lock in savings).

### Q: How much deposit is required?
A: We require 20% at signing to hold your spring slot and lock in pricing. The balance isn't due until installation begins. We also offer financing options if needed.

### Q: Will my pool sit through winter?
A: If you're booking spring installation, we handle design and permits through winter. Installation happens in spring when weather is ideal. If you want a fall installation, we can complete that too - pools handle winter perfectly with proper closing.

### Q: What if prices don't increase in 2027?
A: Based on industry trends, material costs, and demand, prices have increased every year for the past several years. By locking in now, you're protected either way. If prices stay flat, you still got the off-season discount. If they increase (likely), you saved even more.
