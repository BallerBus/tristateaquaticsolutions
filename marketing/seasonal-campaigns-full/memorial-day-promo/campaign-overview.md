# Memorial Day Promotion
## 3-Day Flash Sale Campaign

**Campaign Period:** Friday, May 22 - Monday, May 25, 2026 (Memorial Day Weekend)
**Campaign Theme:** "Memorial Day Pool Sale"
**Primary Goal:** Generate leads and deposits during high-engagement holiday weekend
**Format:** Flash sale with time-limited urgency

---

## Executive Summary

Memorial Day represents a powerful marketing moment - families are together, thinking about summer, and motivated by holiday deals. This 3-day flash sale creates urgency and capitalizes on the emotional peak of early summer anticipation.

---

## Campaign Strategy

### Core Positioning
This is a limited-time event, not a general promotion. The message is clear: extraordinary savings available for 72 hours only. Miss it and it's gone.

### Key Elements
1. **Time-limited urgency** (72 hours only)
2. **Significant discount** ($3,500 off + bonuses)
3. **Summer kickoff emotion** (start of pool season)
4. **Family focus** (holiday gathering theme)

---

## Flash Sale Offer

### "Memorial Day Blitz" Package

**Discount:** $3,500 off installation

**Bonuses:**
- Free premium pool cover ($600 value)
- Free pool startup kit ($400 value)
- Free first-season chemical package ($300 value)

**Total Value:** $4,800+

**Requirements:**
- Signed contract within 72 hours (by Monday 11:59 PM)
- 20% deposit required
- Valid for any 2026 installation
