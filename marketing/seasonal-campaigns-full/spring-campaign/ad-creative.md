## A/B Testing Schedule

### Week 1-2: Headlines
Test 3 headline variations per ad concept
Measure: CTR
Winner: Highest CTR after 1,000 impressions each

### Week 3-4: Creative
Test image vs. video for Facebook/Instagram
Measure: Conversion rate
Winner: Lowest cost per lead

### Week 5-6: Audiences
Test interest-based vs. lookalike audiences
Measure: Lead quality (consultation show rate)
Winner: Highest quality leads

### Week 7-8: Offers
Test LED lighting offer vs. $1,500 discount
Measure: Conversion rate
Winner: Highest conversion rate

## Landing Page Requirements

Each ad should drive to a dedicated landing page with:

1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline matching ad promise
   - Subheadline with key benefit
   - Hero image or video
   - Primary CTA above fold

2. **Social Proof**
   - Star rating
   - Customer testimonials
   - Project count

3. **Offer Details**
   - Clear explanation of spring special
   - Value breakdown
   - Deadline if applicable

4. **Process Overview**
   - Timeline visualization
   - Steps from consultation to swimming

5. **Form**
   - Name, email, phone
   - Address (for site assessment)
   - Preferred timeline
   - How they heard about us

6. **Trust Signals**
   - Licenses and certifications
   - Insurance information
   - BBB rating
   - Manufacturer partnerships
