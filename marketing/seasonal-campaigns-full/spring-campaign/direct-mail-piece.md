## ALTERNATIVE FORMATS

### Option B: Oversized Letter (9x12 Envelope)
- Higher perceived value
- More space for information
- Include brochure and reply card
- Cost: ~$15,000 for 3 drops

### Option C: Dimensional Mailer
- Box with branded item (mini pool float, sunglasses)
- Extremely high open rate
- Very high cost per piece
- Best for high-value prospects only
- Cost: ~$25 per piece (limited quantity)

### Option D: Every Door Direct Mail (EDDM)
- Lower cost per piece
- No personalization
- Broader reach (all addresses in carrier route)
- Less targeted
- Cost: ~$0.25 per piece all-in
