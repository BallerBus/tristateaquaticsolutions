### Demographic Filters
- Homeowners only
- Property value: $600,000+
- Household income: $175,000+
- Length of residence: 3+ years
- Age of homeowner: 35-60
- NO existing pool (satellite/property data)
- Single-family homes only

### Exclusions
- Existing customers
- Addresses with existing pools
- Renters
- Multi-family properties
- Recent movers (<1 year)
