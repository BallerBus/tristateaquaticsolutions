## SECTION 9: FAQ

### Q: Why is fall installation cheaper?
A: Simple economics. Spring is our busiest season - high demand means standard pricing. Fall demand is lower, so we pass savings to customers who book during the off-season.

### Q: Won't my pool freeze?
A: Fiberglass pools handle winter beautifully. We install a professional cover and winterize all equipment. Thousands of pools across the Northeast spend every winter under snow without issue.

### Q: Can I still use the pool in fall?
A: If you have a heater (which we recommend), absolutely. Many of our clients swim into October. The pool is ready when you are.

### Q: What if there's an early freeze?
A: We monitor weather closely and adjust timelines as needed. In our 15+ years, we've never had a project compromised by early winter weather.
