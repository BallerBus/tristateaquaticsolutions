## Compliance Checklist (TCPA)

### Before Sending Any SMS:
- [ ] Recipient has provided express written consent
- [ ] Consent was obtained with clear disclosure of SMS marketing
- [ ] Opt-out mechanism is included in every message
- [ ] Messages are sent only during permitted hours (8 AM - 9 PM local time)
- [ ] Opt-out requests are processed within 24 hours
- [ ] Records of consent are maintained for 4+ years

### Required Elements:
1. **Business Identification:** Every message identifies Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
2. **Opt-Out Instructions:** "Reply STOP to opt out" in every message
3. **Contact Method:** Phone number or link for questions

### Prohibited Practices:
- Sending messages before 8 AM or after 9 PM recipient's local time
- Sending to numbers on Do Not Call list
- Using misleading or deceptive content
- Sending without prior express consent
- Ignoring opt-out requests

## Best Practices Summary

| Category | Best Send Time | Frequency |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| Appointment Reminders | 24-48 hrs before | Per appointment |
| Lead Response | Immediately / within 5 min | As needed |
| Status Updates | Same day as event | Per milestone |
| Promotional | Tue-Thu, 10 AM - 2 PM | Max 4/month |
| Re-engagement | Tue-Wed, 10 AM - 12 PM | Max 2/month per lead |

### Character Count Guidelines:
- **Standard SMS:** 160 characters max for single message
- **With Opt-out:** Keep content under 140 chars to fit opt-out
- **Links:** Use URL shorteners (bit.ly, tinyurl) for tracking

### Response Rate Optimization:
1. Personalize with first name
2. Include clear, single CTA
3. Create appropriate urgency
4. Send at optimal times
5. A/B test messaging variations
