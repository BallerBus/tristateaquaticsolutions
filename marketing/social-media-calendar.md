### Post 17: Saltwater vs Chlorine
**Theme:** Education
**Visual:** Clear pool water close-up or infographic comparison

```
Saltwater vs. Chlorine: Let's Clear Up the Confusion

First, the truth: Saltwater pools ARE chlorine pools. The difference is how the chlorine gets there.

TRADITIONAL CHLORINE
- You add chlorine manually
- Distinct "pool smell"
- Can irritate skin and eyes
- Lower upfront cost
- More hands-on maintenance

SALTWATER SYSTEM
- Salt cell generates chlorine naturally
- Softer, silkier water feel
- Gentler on skin, eyes, and swimsuits
- Higher upfront investment (~$1,500-$2,500)
- Lower chemical costs over time

WHICH IS BETTER?
Neither is "better" - it depends on your preferences.

Want minimal chemical handling and silky water? Go saltwater.
Want lower upfront cost and proven simplicity? Traditional works great.

We install both. We'll help you choose what's right for YOUR family.
```
