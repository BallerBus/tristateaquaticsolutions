## FRONT SIDE (UV COATED)

**Overall Vibe:** Clean, professional, urgent but not tacky. High-contrast and easy to read from a distance.

### [Section 1: TOP - Image & Die-Cut]
- **Area:** Top 3 inches of the hanger.
- **Die-cut:** Standard circular hole for door handle.
- **Background Image:** A high-quality photo of a crystal clear, sparkling pool. The water should be a pleasant **Pool Blue (#4ECDC4)**. The photo should look like a realistic PA/DE backyard, not a tropical resort. The top of the water should be visible below the die-cut.
- **Logo:** Place the TriState Aquatic Solutions logo subtly in the top right corner of this section, over the image.

### [Section 2: HEADLINE]
- **Area:** Immediately below the image section.
- **Text:** "DON'T LET YOUR POOL TURN GREEN üü¢"
- **Font:** Montserrat or Poppins, Extra Bold.
- **Color:** **Deep Navy (#1B365D)**.
- **Size:** Very large, the most prominent text on the front. The green circle emoji should be included and rendered clearly.
- **Layout:** Centered.

### [Section 3: SUBHEADLINE]
- **Area:** Below the main headline.
- **Text:** "Spring Pool Opening Season Is Here. Book now before your neighbor takes your spot."
- **Font:** Open Sans or Lato, Bold.
- **Color:** **Charcoal (#333333)**.
- **Size:** Smaller than the headline but still very readable.
- **Layout:** Centered.
