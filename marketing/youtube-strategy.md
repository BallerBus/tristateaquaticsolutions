## 1. Channel Setup

### Channel Name Options

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **Tri-State Aquatic Solutions** (Recommended) | Brand consistency, professional, searchable | Longer name |
| **Tri-State Pools** | Shorter, memorable | Loses "Aquatic Solutions" brand equity |
| **Main Line Pool Pros** | Geo-targeted, local feel | Limits geographic expansion |
| **TSAS Pools** | Short, unique | Not immediately recognizable |

**Recommendation:** Use "Tri-State Aquatic Solutions" for brand consistency across all platforms.

### Channel Art Specifications

**Banner Image:**
- Dimensions: 2560 x 1440 pixels (safe area: 1546 x 423 pixels center)
- Content: High-quality pool photo with text overlay
- Text: "Tri-State Aquatic Solutions" + "Your Backyard, Elevated" + "Fiberglass Pool Experts"
- Colors: Deep Navy (#0E1F34) background areas, Aqua (#00A6B2) accents, White text
- Include website URL in safe area

**Banner Design Elements:**
- Left side: Beautiful completed pool photo (golden hour lighting)
- Center: Logo + tagline
- Right side: "Subscribe for Pool Tips" + social icons

### Profile Image Guidance

**Specifications:**
- Dimensions: 800 x 800 pixels (displays as circle)
- Content: Logo mark only (not full wordmark)
- Background: Deep Navy (#0E1F34) or transparent
- Ensure logo is centered and visible at small sizes
- Test at 98 x 98 pixels (how it appears in comments)

**Design Tips:**
- Avoid text in profile image (illegible at small sizes)
- Use the wave/water mark element from brand identity
- High contrast for visibility
- Consistent with other social platform profile images

### YouTube SEO Best Practices

#### Title Optimization
1. **Front-load keywords** - Put most important words first
2. **Keep it under 60 characters** - Prevents truncation
3. **Use numbers when applicable** - "5 Tips", "2026 Guide"
4. **Add emotional triggers** - "Incredible", "Complete", "Essential"
5. **Match search intent** - Use words people actually search

#### Description Best Practices
1. **First 150 characters are critical** - Shown in search results
2. **Include timestamps** - Improves watch time and SEO
3. **Add 3-5 relevant links** - Website, related videos, resources
4. **Write 250+ words** - More content for YouTube to index
5. **Use keywords naturally** - 3-5 times throughout
6. **Include CTA** - Tell viewers what to do next

#### Thumbnail Best Practices
1. **High contrast colors** - Stand out in feed
2. **Large, readable text** - 3-5 words maximum
3. **Human faces/emotions** - Increase click-through rate
4. **Consistent branding** - Recognizable style
5. **Test at small size** - Most viewers see thumbnails on mobile

#### Tag Strategy
1. **Primary keyword first** - Most important tag
2. **Include exact match** - "fiberglass pool installation"
3. **Include phrase match** - "how to install fiberglass pool"
4. **Add location tags** - "main line pa", "chester county"
5. **Use 8-15 tags** - Quality over quantity
6. **Include brand name** - "Tri-State Aquatic Solutions"

#### Watch Time Optimization
1. **Hook in first 15 seconds** - State the value immediately
2. **Use pattern interrupts** - Change visuals every 30-60 seconds
3. **Add timestamps** - Viewers can navigate, improving session time
4. **Create series content** - Playlists increase total watch time
5. **End with strong CTA** - Ask for like, subscribe, and next video

### Community Tab Engagement Strategies

#### Posting Schedule
| Day | Post Type | Best Time |
|-----|-----------|-----------|
| Monday | Engagement (poll/question) | 12pm EST |
| Wednesday | Educational (tip/fact) | 2pm EST |
| Friday | New video announcement | 10am EST |
| Sunday | Behind-the-scenes/casual | 6pm EST |

#### Engagement Best Practices

1. **Reply to every comment** within 24 hours
2. **Heart comments** that are helpful or positive
3. **Pin the best comment** to encourage more engagement
4. **Ask follow-up questions** to continue conversation
5. **Use polls** at least once per week (highest engagement)
6. **Include images** in most posts (better visibility)
7. **Cross-promote videos** naturally, not every post

#### Content Mix Guidelines
- 40% Engagement posts (polls, questions, contests)
- 30% Educational posts (tips, facts, myth busters)
- 20% Promotional posts (videos, offers, testimonials)
- 10% Behind-the-scenes (team, projects, casual)

#### Metrics to Track
- Poll participation rate
- Comment count per post
- Click-through rate on links
- Subscriber growth correlation
