## Margin Targets

### Project Category Targets

| Category | Target Gross Margin | Minimum Acceptable |
|----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Complete Pool Installation | 35% - 40% | 30% |
| Equipment Only Sales | 25% - 30% | 20% |
| Decking/Hardscape | 30% - 35% | 25% |
| Water Features | 35% - 45% | 30% |
| Renovation Projects | 40% - 50% | 35% |
| Service/Maintenance | 45% - 55% | 40% |

### Pricing Formula

```
Selling Price = (Total Cost) / (1 - Target Margin)

Example:
Total Cost = $45,000
Target Margin = 35%
Selling Price = $45,000 / (1 - 0.35) = $69,231

Round to: $69,500 or $70,000
```

### Cost Components to Include

- Materials (shells, equipment, supplies)
- Labor (direct and supervision)
- Subcontractor costs
- Equipment rental
- Permits and inspections
- Delivery and freight
- Overhead allocation (15% of direct costs)
- Contingency (5% for standard, 10% for complex)

## Discount Approval Levels

### Standard Discount Authority

| Role | Maximum Discount | Conditions |
|------|------------------|------------|
| Sales Representative | 3% | Any project |
| Sales Manager | 7% | Must document reason |
| General Manager | 12% | Written approval required |
| Owner | 15%+ | Executive approval only |

### Pre-Approved Discount Scenarios

| Scenario | Automatic Discount | Approval Level |
|----------|-------------------|----------------|
| Cash Payment (full) | 3% | Sales Rep |
| Military/First Responder | 5% | Sales Rep |
| Repeat Customer | 5% | Sales Rep |
| Customer Referral (referred) | 3% | Sales Rep |
| Model Home/Display | 10% - 20% | GM/Owner |
| Off-Season Booking (Nov-Feb) | 5% - 8% | Sales Manager |
| Multiple Pool Purchase | 8% - 12% | GM |

### Discount Documentation Requirements

All discounts over 3% require:

1. **Written justification** in CRM notes
2. **Approval signature** (email acceptable)
3. **Final margin verification** before contract signing
4. **Discount code** on invoice for tracking

### Prohibited Discount Practices

- Discounting to match unverified competitor quotes
- Discounting below minimum margin without owner approval
- Verbal discount promises without documentation
- Discounting permits, inspections, or safety equipment
- Stacking multiple discounts without approval

## Price Adjustment Guidelines

### When to Adjust Pricing

**Increase pricing when:**
- Material costs increase >5%
- Labor costs increase significantly
- High demand/capacity constraints
- Specialty or rush projects
- Difficult site conditions

**Site Condition Surcharges:**

| Condition | Surcharge |
|-----------|-----------|
| Restricted Access | $1,500 - $5,000 |
| Rock Excavation | $50 - $150 per cubic yard |
| High Water Table | $2,000 - $5,000 |
| Steep Grade (>10%) | $2,500 - $7,500 |
| Long Distance from Panel | $25 per additional foot |
| Tree/Stump Removal | $500 - $2,000 per tree |

### Quote Validity

- Standard quotes valid for **30 days**
- Complex projects valid for **14 days**
- Material pricing subject to change after validity period
- Permit fee changes passed through at cost

### Annual Price Review

- Full pricing review conducted each **January**
- Mid-year adjustments if material costs change >10%
- New pricing effective for quotes issued after approval date
- Existing contracts honored at original pricing
