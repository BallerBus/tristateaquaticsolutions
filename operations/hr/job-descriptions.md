## Pool Installation Technician

### Position Overview

**Department:** Operations / Field Services
**Reports To:** Lead Installer / Foreman
**FLSA Status:** Non-Exempt (Hourly)
**Work Schedule:** Full-Time, Monday-Friday (some weekends during peak season)
**Location:** Field-based with daily reporting to job sites

### Position Summary

The Pool Installation Technician is responsible for the physical installation of swimming pools, including excavation preparation, plumbing, electrical rough-in assistance, liner installation, decking, and equipment setup. This position requires strong technical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work effectively as part of a team in varying outdoor conditions.

### Essential Responsibilities

**Installation Work (70%)**
- Assist with site preparation including layout, grading, and excavation support
- Install pool walls, frames, and structural components per specifications
- Perform plumbing installation including pipes, fittings, skimmers, and returns
- Install vinyl liners, ensuring proper fit and wrinkle-free application
- Set up and connect pool equipment (pumps, filters, heaters, chlorinators)
- Install pool decking, coping, and finishing materials
- Perform concrete work including pouring and finishing pool decks
- Install safety equipment (fencing, covers, alarms) per code requirements

**Quality and Safety (20%)**
- Follow all safety protocols and wear required PPE
- Perform work according to company quality standards
- Ensure installations meet local building codes and permit requirements
- Keep job sites clean, organized, and safe
- Report any safety hazards or concerns immediately
- Participate in daily safety briefings and weekly safety meetings

**Support Activities (10%)**
- Load and unload materials and equipment
- Maintain tools and equipment in good working condition
- Complete daily work logs and time sheets accurately
- Communicate with team members and supervisors effectively
- Assist other crew members as needed

### Required Qualifications

**Education and Experience:**
- High school diploma or GED required
- 1+ years of construction or trades experience preferred
- Pool installation experience a plus but not required
- Valid driver's license with clean driving record

**Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:**
- Basic plumbing and electrical knowledge
- Ability to read and interpret blueprints and technical drawings
- Proficiency with hand and power tools
- Strong mechanical aptitude
- Physical ability to perform demanding outdoor work
- Ability to work in extreme weather conditions
- Strong work ethic and reliability
- Team player with positive attitude
- Basic math skills for measurements and calculations

**Physical Requirements:**
- Lift and carry up to 75 pounds regularly
- Stand, walk, kneel, and crouch for extended periods
- Work in trenches and excavations
- Climb ladders and work at heights
- Work outdoors in all weather conditions

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Quality Score | 95%+ | Quality inspection pass rate |
| Safety Compliance | 100% | No safety violations or incidents |
| Attendance | 95%+ | Days present vs. scheduled |
| On-Time Completion | 90%+ | Tasks completed within estimated time |
| Customer Feedback | 4.5+/5 | Post-installation surveys |
| Tool/Equipment Care | 100% | Proper maintenance and no damage |

### Compensation

**Pay Range:** $18.00 - $28.00 per hour (based on experience)
**Overtime:** Time and a half for hours over 40/week
**Bonuses:** Eligible for project completion and performance bonuses

## Lead Installer / Foreman

### Position Overview

**Department:** Operations / Field Services
**Reports To:** Project Manager
**FLSA Status:** Non-Exempt (Hourly)
**Work Schedule:** Full-Time, Monday-Friday (weekends as needed)
**Location:** Field-based, multiple job sites

### Position Summary

The Lead Installer / Foreman oversees pool installation crews and ensures projects are completed safely, on time, and to company quality standards. This position serves as the on-site supervisor, responsible for crew leadership, quality control, customer communication, and coordination with project management.

### Essential Responsibilities

**Crew Leadership (40%)**
- Supervise and direct crew of 2-5 installation technicians
- Conduct daily job briefings including safety talks and work assignments
- Train and mentor new technicians
- Monitor crew performance and provide feedback
- Ensure crew maintains professional appearance and behavior
- Address and resolve on-site personnel issues
- Coordinate crew schedules and time-off requests

**Project Execution (35%)**
- Review project plans and specifications before work begins
- Ensure accurate layout and execution of installations
- Perform quality inspections throughout the installation process
- Make technical decisions and solve problems on-site
- Coordinate with subcontractors (electricians, concrete, landscaping)
- Ensure all work meets code requirements and company standards
- Manage on-site materials and equipment

**Communication and Documentation (15%)**
- Serve as primary on-site contact for customers
- Provide project updates to Project Manager
- Document daily progress with photos and notes
- Complete all required paperwork and reports
- Communicate material needs and delivery requirements
- Report any issues, delays, or changes immediately

**Safety Management (10%)**
- Enforce all safety policies and procedures on-site
- Conduct job site safety inspections
- Lead daily toolbox talks
- Investigate and report any incidents or near-misses
- Ensure all crew members use required PPE
- Identify and mitigate potential hazards

### Required Qualifications

**Education and Experience:**
- High school diploma or GED required
- 3+ years of pool installation experience
- 1+ years of supervisory or crew lead experience
- Valid driver's license with clean driving record
- CPO (Certified Pool Operator) certification preferred
- OSHA 10 or 30 certification preferred

**Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:**
- Expert knowledge of pool installation techniques
- Proficient in plumbing, basic electrical, and concrete work
- Strong blueprint and technical drawing interpretation
- Leadership and team management skills
- Excellent customer service and communication skills
- Problem-solving and decision-making ability
- Time management and organizational skills
- Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
- Basic computer skills for reporting and communication

**Physical Requirements:**
- Lift and carry up to 75 pounds regularly
- Perform physical labor in addition to supervision
- Work outdoors in all weather conditions
- Stand, walk, and move throughout job sites

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Project Quality Score | 97%+ | Quality inspection pass rate |
| On-Time Completion | 95%+ | Projects completed on schedule |
| Budget Adherence | Within 5% | Actual vs. estimated labor hours |
| Safety Record | Zero incidents | Lost-time injuries and violations |
| Customer Satisfaction | 4.7+/5 | Post-installation surveys |
| Crew Retention | 85%+ | Annual retention rate for direct reports |
| Rework Rate | <3% | Callbacks within 30 days |

### Compensation

**Pay Range:** $25.00 - $38.00 per hour (based on experience)
**Overtime:** Time and a half for hours over 40/week
**Bonuses:** Eligible for project, safety, and annual performance bonuses
**Vehicle:** Company vehicle provided for work use

## Project Manager

### Position Overview

**Department:** Operations
**Reports To:** Operations Director / General Manager
**FLSA Status:** Exempt (Salaried)
**Work Schedule:** Full-Time, Monday-Friday (flexibility required)
**Location:** Office-based with frequent site visits

### Position Summary

The Project Manager is responsible for planning, coordinating, and overseeing pool installation projects from contract signing through final completion and customer handoff. This position ensures projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to customer expectations while maintaining company quality and safety standards.

### Essential Responsibilities

**Project Planning (25%)**
- Review contracts and develop detailed project plans
- Create project schedules and resource allocation plans
- Order materials and coordinate delivery schedules
- Obtain necessary permits and inspections
- Identify potential risks and develop mitigation strategies
- Conduct pre-construction meetings with crews and customers

**Project Execution and Monitoring (35%)**
- Oversee multiple concurrent installation projects
- Monitor project progress against schedule and budget
- Conduct regular site visits to verify quality and progress
- Coordinate subcontractors and specialty trades
- Manage project changes and change orders
- Ensure compliance with building codes and permits
- Resolve technical issues and make project decisions

**Customer Management (20%)**
- Serve as primary customer contact throughout project
- Conduct project kick-off meetings with customers
- Provide regular progress updates and status reports
- Address and resolve customer concerns promptly
- Conduct final walkthrough and project handoff
- Ensure customer satisfaction before project close-out

**Team Coordination (15%)**
- Coordinate with sales team on project requirements
- Assign crews and resources to projects
- Support and guide Lead Installers/Foremen
- Participate in hiring and training of field staff
- Conduct project post-mortems and lessons learned

**Administrative (5%)**
- Maintain accurate project documentation
- Update project management software
- Prepare project status reports for management
- Manage project budgets and track costs
- Process invoices and approve timesheets

### Required Qualifications

**Education and Experience:**
- Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Business, or related field (or equivalent experience)
- 5+ years of construction or project management experience
- 2+ years of pool/aquatic industry experience preferred
- PMP certification preferred
- Valid driver's license

**Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:**
- Strong project management methodology knowledge
- Proficient in project management software (MS Project, Buildertrend, or similar)
- Excellent customer relationship management skills
- Strong leadership and team coordination abilities
- Budget management and financial acumen
- Contract interpretation and management
- Problem-solving and critical thinking skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite
- Knowledge of local building codes and permit processes

**Physical Requirements:**
- Ability to visit job sites regularly
- Walk through construction sites including uneven terrain
- Occasional lifting up to 30 pounds

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| On-Time Delivery | 95%+ | Projects completed by scheduled date |
| Budget Performance | Within 5% | Actual vs. budgeted costs |
| Customer Satisfaction | 4.8+/5 | NPS and post-project surveys |
| Quality Score | 98%+ | Final inspection pass rate |
| Safety Performance | Zero incidents | Lost-time injuries on managed projects |
| Warranty Claims | <2% | Claims within first year |
| Active Project Capacity | 10-15 | Concurrent projects managed |

### Compensation

**Salary Range:** $60,000 - $85,000 annually (based on experience)
**Bonuses:** Eligible for quarterly and annual performance bonuses (up to 15% of salary)
**Vehicle:** Company vehicle or vehicle allowance provided

## Sales Consultant

### Position Overview

**Department:** Sales
**Reports To:** Sales Manager / General Manager
**FLSA Status:** Non-Exempt (Base + Commission)
**Work Schedule:** Full-Time, flexible schedule including evenings and weekends
**Location:** Office and field-based (customer locations)

### Position Summary

The Sales Consultant is responsible for generating new business by meeting with prospective customers, assessing their needs, designing pool solutions, and closing sales. This position requires strong relationship-building skills, technical knowledge of pool products and installation, and the ability to guide customers through the buying process.

### Essential Responsibilities

**Sales Activities (60%)**
- Respond to leads and inquiries within 24 hours
- Conduct initial phone consultations to qualify prospects
- Schedule and conduct in-home consultations
- Assess customer needs, preferences, and site conditions
- Design customized pool solutions using design software
- Prepare detailed proposals and pricing
- Present solutions and handle objections effectively
- Close sales and obtain signed contracts
- Collect deposits and initial payments
- Meet or exceed monthly and annual sales targets

**Customer Relationship (20%)**
- Build rapport and trust with prospective customers
- Educate customers on pool options, features, and benefits
- Follow up with leads who haven't committed
- Maintain ongoing communication during the sales cycle
- Hand off sold projects to operations team smoothly
- Request referrals and reviews from satisfied customers

**Market Development (10%)**
- Attend home shows, trade events, and community events
- Build relationships with builders and contractors
- Generate self-sourced leads through networking
- Contribute to marketing initiatives and campaigns
- Stay informed on competitor offerings and pricing

**Administration (10%)**
- Maintain accurate records in CRM system
- Prepare accurate contracts and documentation
- Update pipeline and forecast reports
- Participate in sales meetings and training
- Complete expense reports and administrative tasks

### Required Qualifications

**Education and Experience:**
- High school diploma required; Bachelor's degree preferred
- 2+ years of sales experience (construction, home improvement, or high-value products preferred)
- Pool industry experience a plus
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation

**Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:**
- Strong sales and closing skills
- Excellent interpersonal and communication abilities
- Customer-focused with consultative selling approach
- Technical aptitude to learn pool products and installation
- Proficiency with CRM software and design tools
- Self-motivated with strong work ethic
- Professional appearance and demeanor
- Ability to work flexible hours including evenings/weekends
- Comfortable with technology and digital presentations

**Physical Requirements:**
- Travel to customer locations (25-50% travel time)
- Walk through various property conditions
- Occasionally lift displays or samples (up to 30 pounds)

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Closed | $750K+ annually | Total contract value |
| Closing Rate | 25%+ | Contracts signed / consultations |
| Average Deal Size | $45K+ | Average contract value |
| Lead Response Time | <4 hours | Time from inquiry to first contact |
| Consultations Conducted | 15+/month | In-home appointments completed |
| Customer Satisfaction | 4.5+/5 | Post-sales survey scores |
| CRM Compliance | 100% | All activities logged accurately |

### Compensation

**Base Salary:** $40,000 - $50,000 annually
**Commission:** 3-5% of contract value (uncapped)
**On-Target Earnings (OTE):** $80,000 - $120,000+
**Bonuses:** Additional bonuses for exceeding targets
**Benefits:** Full benefits package
**Vehicle:** Mileage reimbursement provided
