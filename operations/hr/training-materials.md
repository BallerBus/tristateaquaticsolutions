## Training Program Overview

### Training Philosophy

At Tri-State Aquatic Solutions, we believe that well-trained employees are the foundation of quality work, customer satisfaction, and workplace safety. Our comprehensive training program ensures every team member has the knowledge and skills to excel in their role while maintaining our standards of excellence.

### Training Principles

1. **Safety First** - All employees complete safety training before beginning work
2. **Hands-On Learning** - Theory combined with practical application
3. **Continuous Development** - Ongoing training throughout employment
4. **Role Relevance** - Training tailored to specific job requirements
5. **Measurable Outcomes** - Assessments to verify competency
6. **Documentation** - All training tracked and certified

### Training Categories

| Category | Description | Mandatory |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Safety | Workplace safety, OSHA compliance, PPE | Yes |
| Technical | Job-specific skills and knowledge | Yes |
| Customer Service | Customer interaction and service excellence | Yes |
| Sales | Selling skills and product knowledge | Sales team |
| Software/Systems | Company technology and tools | Yes |
| Leadership | Management and supervision skills | Managers |
| Professional Development | Career growth and advancement | Optional |

### Training Delivery Methods

- **Classroom Training** - Instructor-led sessions
- **Hands-On Training** - Practical skills development
- **Online Modules** - Self-paced e-learning
- **Video Training** - Demonstration and instruction videos
- **Shadowing** - Learning by observation
- **Mentorship** - One-on-one guidance
- **Field Training** - On-the-job learning
- **Certification Programs** - External certifications
