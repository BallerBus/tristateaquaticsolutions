## Pricing Notes

1. **Travel Fees:** No additional travel fee within 25 miles of service area center. $2/mile beyond.

2. **Emergency Service:** After-hours emergency calls (nights/weekends) carry a $75 emergency fee plus standard service rates.

3. **Parts & Equipment:** All parts and equipment are additional unless specified. We provide itemized quotes before any work begins.

4. **Estimates:** Written estimates provided for all work over $200. Estimates valid for 30 days.

5. **Payment Terms:**
   - Maintenance plans: Monthly auto-pay or annual prepay
   - Service calls: Due upon completion
   - Projects over $1,000: 50% deposit, balance upon completion

6. **Warranty:** All labor carries a 90-day warranty. Equipment warranty per manufacturer terms.

7. **Maintenance Customer Discounts:** Active maintenance plan customers receive 10-15% off repairs (based on tier).
