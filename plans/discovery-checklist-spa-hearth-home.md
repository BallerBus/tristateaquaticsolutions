## Red Flags Checklist

**Watch for these warning signs:**

- [ ] Can't or won't provide complete customer contact info
- [ ] No documentation of service history
- [ ] Vague answers about customer count ("around 50" vs "47")
- [ ] Large percentage of "problem" pools
- [ ] Multiple customers already shopping alternatives
- [ ] Outstanding complaints he's hoping we'll inherit
- [ ] Significant unpaid AR he expects us to collect
- [ ] Vague or evasive about customer satisfaction
- [ ] Unwilling to sign non-compete
- [ ] Pressuring for quick close without due diligence
- [ ] Customers haven't been serviced recently
- [ ] No written contracts with customers
- [ ] Can't explain why he's selling these specific customers
- [ ] Revenue numbers don't add up
- [ ] Won't introduce us to customers before closing
- [ ] Employees who serviced customers have already left
- [ ] Equipment records don't exist
- [ ] Multiple customers owed credits or prepaid services
