## Quick Comparison Table

| Factor | Fiberglass | Concrete |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Installation Time | 2-4 weeks | 3-6 months |
| Upfront Cost | $$ | $$$ |

Fiberglass pools are manufactured off-site and installed as a single piece. This means:
- No waiting for concrete to cure
- Less disruption to your yard
- Swimming in weeks, not months
- Weather has less impact on timeline

Concrete pools are built on-site, which means:
- Excavation, framing, steel, gunite, curing
- Multiple contractor visits
- Weather delays common
- Typical timeline: 3-6 months minimum

## Long-Term Costs

[Detailed comparison of maintenance, resurfacing, chemical usage, etc.]

## Which is Right for You?

Choose Fiberglass if:
- You want to swim THIS summer
- You prefer lower maintenance
- You want a smooth surface
- You value warranty protection

Choose Concrete if:
- You need a completely custom shape
- You want a very large pool (40+ feet)
- Timeline isn't a concern
- You're prepared for higher maintenance

For most Main Line and Delaware families, fiberglass offers the best combination of quality, value, and convenience. The faster installation, lower maintenance, and lifetime warranty make it the smart choice for today's homeowners.

## 6. Ethical Guidelines

### What We WILL Do:
- Bid on competitor brand names (legal and common)
- Create honest comparison content
- Highlight our genuine advantages
- Share real customer testimonials about their experience
- Position against competitor weaknesses with facts

### What We WON'T Do:
- Make false claims about competitors
- Use competitor logos or trademarks in ads
- Claim to be the competitor
- Directly defame or attack competitors by name in content
- Encourage fake negative reviews of competitors

### Ad Policy Compliance:
- No competitor trademarks in ad text
- Clear identification as Tri-State Aquatic Solutions
- Landing pages clearly identify us, not competitor
- No misleading claims or comparisons
