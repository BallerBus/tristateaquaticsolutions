## Executive Summary

This research supports Tri-State Aquatic Solutions' expansion from pool maintenance into fiberglass/pre-manufactured pool installation. The Main Line PA and Northern Delaware markets present strong opportunities due to high household incomes, premium home values, and growing demand for smaller "plunge pools" as an affordable luxury alternative.

**Key Findings:**
- Main Line PA median family income: $192,630 (2.2x state average)
- Median home values: $350,000-$556,000 across target counties
- Fiberglass pool installation profit margins: 35% gross / 7% net
- Plunge pool market growing at 7.1% CAGR
- Peak selling season: Late May to early June
- Typical installed price range: $45,000-$85,000 for standard fiberglass pools

## 4. Licensing & Permits (PA & DE)

### 4.1 Pennsylvania Requirements

**Building Permit Required:** Any body of water >24 inches deep

#### Required Permits

| Permit Type | Cost Range | Purpose |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| Building Permit | $100-$500 | Pool structure, decking, fencing |
| Zoning Permit | $50-$200 | Setback compliance (5-15 ft from property lines) |
| Electrical Permit | $75-$300 | Pool electrical systems |
| Plumbing Permit | $50-$150 | Filtration, drainage |
| Environmental Permit | $200-$1,000+ | Near wetlands/streams |
| Fencing Permit | Varies | Required in most municipalities |

**Total Permit Budget:** $500-$2,000

#### Application Requirements
- Two sets of construction drawings with plot plans
- Property dimensions and lot lines
- Existing and proposed structures
- Pool equipment locations
- Contractor worker's compensation insurance proof

**Chester County Note:** Properties near Brandywine Creek often require DEP approval due to flood plain regulations.

### 4.2 Contractor Licensing

Pennsylvania does not require a statewide contractor license for pool installation. However:
- Local municipalities may require business licenses
- Worker's compensation insurance required
- Electrical work requires licensed electrician
- Plumbing work may require licensed plumber
