## Profit Margins

### Industry Benchmarks
- **Gross Profit Margin**: 35% (vs 15-20% for general construction)
- **Net Profit Margin**: 7% of sales
- **High-margin add-ons**: 50-60%

### Cost Breakdown ($70,000 project)
| Category | % | Amount |
|----------|---|--------|
| Materials (shell, equipment, plumbing) | 45% | $31,500 |
| Labor (crew, subcontractors) | 30% | $21,000 |
| Overhead (insurance, permits, admin) | 10% | $7,000 |
| **Gross Profit** | 15-25% | $10,500-$17,500 |
| Marketing & Acquisition | 5% | $3,500 |
| **Net Profit** | 5-10% | $3,500-$7,000 |

## Becoming a Dealer

### Requirements
- General contractor experience accepted
- Landscaping/hardscaping background accepted
- Excavation experience accepted
- Existing pool builders welcome

### Process
1. Submit dealer inquiry (most have online forms)
2. Background/experience evaluation
3. Training program completion (typically 1 week)
4. Territory assignment
5. Begin ordering

### Notes
- Wholesale pricing disclosed only during onboarding
- Minimum orders vary by manufacturer
- Payment terms negotiated individually
