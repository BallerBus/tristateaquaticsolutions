## Pennsylvania Requirements

### Home Improvement Contractor (HIC) Registration
- **Registration Fee**: $50 (non-refundable)
- **Validity**: 2 years (biennial renewal)
- **Threshold**: Required if performing $5,000+ of work annually
- **Apply Online**: https://hic.attorneygeneral.gov
- **Contact**: PA Attorney General 888-520-6680

### Required Information for Application
- Business details and description of services
- Insurance certificates
- Prior registrations in other states
- Principal/shareholder information
- Business address and telephone

### Post-Registration Requirements
- Display HIC registration number on ALL advertisements, contracts, estimates, proposals
- Update Attorney General within 30 days of any business changes

### Insurance Minimums (Philadelphia standards)
| Coverage Type | Minimum |
|--------------|---------|
| General Liability | $500,000 per occurrence |
| Automobile Liability | $300,000 |
| Workers Comp (per accident) | $100,000 |
| Workers Comp (per employee) | $100,000 |
| Workers Comp (policy limit) | $500,000 |

### Building Permits
- Vary by municipality
- Typically require zoning approval, setback compliance, safety inspections
- Any water body >24" deep requires building permit
- Total permit costs: $500-$2,000
- Required: Building, Zoning, Electrical, Plumbing permits
- Chester County properties near Brandywine Creek may need DEP approval

## Delaware Requirements

### Contractor Registration
- **Registration Fee**: $75/year (prorated first year)
- Register with Delaware Division of Revenue
- Register with Delaware Department of Labor under Chapter 36

### Required Coverage
- Workers compensation insurance
- Liability insurance
- Unemployment insurance

### Non-Resident Contractors
- Must secure a contractor's surety bond

### Electrical Work
- Pool electrical must be performed by Delaware-licensed electrician

### Building Permits
- Plan review required for new pools
- Permit required for pools 24+ inches in height
- Public pool permits valid April 15 to April 15

## HICPA Contract Requirements (Pennsylvania)

### Contracts MUST Include
- Contractor name, street address (NOT P.O. Box), phone, HIC registration number
- All known subcontractor names, addresses, phone numbers
- Specific start and completion dates
- Detailed scope of work, materials, specifications
- Total contract price
- Proof of liability insurance ($50,000 personal injury / $50,000 property damage minimum)
- Attorney General contact: 800-441-2555

### Deposit Limits (PA Law)
**For contracts over $1,000:**
- Down payment CANNOT exceed 1/3 of total price
- Plus cost of special-order materials (must be itemized)

### Penalties for Violations
- 3x damages plus homeowner's attorney fees and court costs
- Criminal prosecution possible
- Registration revocation
- Contract becomes unenforceable

## Delaware Contract Requirements

### Written Contract Must Include
- Detailed list of all materials
- Approximate completion date (or disclosure if none provided)
- Total contract price
- Disclosure if cost-plus basis with calculation method
- Cancellation rights notice

### Cancellation Rights
- 3 business days for contracts over $500
- 5 business days for buyers over age 62 or with disability

### Permit Application Deadlines
- Within 30 days after 50% of deposit is paid, OR
- Within 30 days after contract signing if no deposit
