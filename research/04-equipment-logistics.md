## Insurance Costs

### General Liability
- **Annual Cost**: $804-$1,068/year ($67-$89/month)
- $1,000,000 per occurrence
- $2,000,000 aggregate
- $5,000 medical payments
- $250 deductible

### Recommended Additional Coverage
- Pool pop-up coverage (water table damage)
- Pollution coverage (chlorine, chemicals)
- Commercial auto insurance
- Inland marine (tools in transport)
- Equipment breakdown coverage
- Umbrella liability

### Workers Compensation
- **Annual Cost**: $400-$3,000/year
- Classification Code: 5223 (Swimming Pool Construction)
- Note: 13.1% rate increase in 2024/2025
