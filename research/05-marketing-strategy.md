## Free/Low-Cost Marketing Channels

### Nextdoor Marketing (Free - $75/deal)
- 35% month-over-month growth in word-of-mouth within 3 months
- 67% of users share recommendations
- Post bi-weekly minimum with conversational tone
- Comment aggressively on posts for visibility

### Facebook Community Groups (Free)
- Join 3-5 active local community groups in affluent areas
- Share informational content weekly
- Build relationships rather than selling
- Respond helpfully when members ask for recommendations

### Referral Program Structure
| Referral Source | Commission |
|-----------------|------------|
| Existing customers | $500-$1,000 cash |
| Partners (landscapers, realtors) | 20% ($10,000 on $50K job) |

### Yard Signs ROI
- Cost: $15-20 per sign
- Leads: 3-5 calls per sign over 30-60 days
- Cost per lead: $4-7
- ROI: 1,000%+ possible on pool jobs

### Vehicle Wraps ROI
- Cost: $2,500-5,000 for full wrap
- Lifespan: 5-7 years
- Daily impressions: 30,000-70,000
- ROI: Up to 800% over lifespan
- 38x more impressions per dollar than traditional advertising

### Review Generation
- Ask within 24 hours of job completion (not at invoice)
- Use direct Google review links
- Target 3-5 new reviews per month
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)

## Quick-Win Tactics (15-Day Plan)

### Days 1-3: Partnership Activation
- Contact landscapers, real estate agents, builders
- Offer 20% commission on referrals
- Host partnership lunch event

### Days 4-7: Lead Generation
- Launch free pool inspection offers ($250 value)
- Direct mail to luxury zip codes
- Implement deposit incentives:
  - "Spring Lock-in": 25% deposit for $5,000-7,500 discount
  - "Dual Deposit": 15% now, 10% at permit approval

### Days 8-15: Urgency Messaging
- Create scarcity: "3 spring slots remaining"
- Email sequence to inspection attendees
- Activate partner referrals

### Expected Results
- Day 7: 2-3 qualified inspections
- Day 12: 1 deposit commitment
- Day 15: 3-5 leads from partner referrals
