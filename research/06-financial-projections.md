## Payment Schedule Structure

### Industry Standard Milestone-Based
| Milestone | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| Contract Signing (Deposit) | 15-20% |
| Day of Excavation | 25% |
| Pool Delivery/Set | 25% |
| Day of Decking/Plumbing | 25% |
| Final Completion | 5-10% |

### PA Legal Limit
- Deposit CANNOT exceed 1/3 of total contract price
- Plus special-order materials (itemized)
- 50% deposit NOT legal in PA for contracts over $1,000

### Recommended Structure
- 33% deposit + itemized material costs
- Remaining payments at milestones
- Compliant with PA HICPA law
