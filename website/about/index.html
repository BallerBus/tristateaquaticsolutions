About Tri-State Aquatic Solutions

Where expert craftsmanship meets modern innovation. We are your neighbors, committed to creating backyard retreats where families make memories for generations.

Our Story

From Maintenance to Masterpiece

Tri-State Aquatic Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide pool owners with the exceptional care their investments deserve. For years, we served families across the Main Line and Northern Delaware, maintaining and servicing their pools with the attention to detail that became our calling card.

As we worked alongside homeowners, we listened. We heard the frustrations with pool builders who disappeared after installation. We saw the results of rushed construction and corner-cutting. And we knew we could do better.

Today, Tri-State Aquatic Solutions has grown into a full-service pool installation company. We bring the same meticulous care that defined our maintenance services to every new pool we build. Our deep understanding of pool systems, gained through years of hands-on service work, informs every design decision we make.

Based in Hockessin, Delaware, we serve communities throughout the Main Line Philadelphia area and Northern Delaware. We are not a national franchise. We are your neighbors, and we build pools where our own families swim.

Our Approach

Local Expertise, Premium Results

From Hockessin to Haverford, from Villanova to Wilmington, we know your neighborhoods. We understand the local codes, the soil conditions, and the architectural character that makes each community unique.

This deep local knowledge, combined with our commitment to modern pool technology, allows us to deliver results that national chains simply cannot match. We do not follow a one-size-fits-all playbook. We design each pool specifically for your property, your lifestyle, and your vision.

Our team specializes in fiberglass, concrete, and vinyl liner pools, giving you options that suit your budget and your goals. Whether you want a quick installation fiberglass pool or a custom concrete masterpiece, we bring the same level of care and expertise to every project.
