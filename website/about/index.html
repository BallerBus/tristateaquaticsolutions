Our Story

From Passion to Premium Pools

Tri-State Aquatic Solutions began with a simple mission: to bring exceptional fiberglass pool installations to homeowners who deserve the best. Operating under Dude Venture Services LLC, we started serving families across the Main Line and Northern Delaware with a commitment to quality that sets us apart.

As we worked alongside homeowners, we listened. We heard frustrations with pool builders who disappeared after installation. We saw the results of rushed construction and corner-cutting. We knew there was a better way.

"We do not just install pools. We create backyard destinations where your family will gather for decades to come."

Today, Tri-State Aquatic Solutions is recognized as the premier fiberglass pool installer in our region. We specialize exclusively in fiberglass pools because we believe they offer the best combination of durability, beauty, and value. Our deep understanding of fiberglass technology, combined with manufacturer partnerships with Latham Pools and Thursday Pools, allows us to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Based in Hockessin, Delaware, we serve communities throughout Chester County, Delaware County, Montgomery County, and New Castle County. We are not a national franchise. We are your neighbors, and we build pools where our own families swim.

Our Specialization

Why We Focus on Fiberglass

After years of industry experience, we chose to specialize in fiberglass pools for compelling reasons that benefit every homeowner we serve.

Faster Installation

Fiberglass pools install in 2-4 weeks, not months. Your backyard transformation happens quickly, with minimal disruption to your daily life. Factory-built shells arrive ready to install, eliminating lengthy on-site construction.

Lifetime Durability

The smooth gelcoat surface resists algae, staining, and chemical damage. No liner replacements, no resurfacing for decades. Fiberglass pools come with structural warranties of 25 years or more because they are built to last.

Lower Lifetime Cost

Reduced chemical usage, minimal maintenance, and no major repairs mean fiberglass pools cost significantly less to own over their lifetime. Many owners save thousands of dollars compared to concrete alternatives.

Superior Comfort

The smooth, non-abrasive surface is gentle on skin and swimsuits. No rough surfaces, no scraped feet, no fading swim trunks. Your family enjoys every swim in comfort.
