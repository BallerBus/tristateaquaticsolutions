8-Step Process

Your Pool Installation Journey

Every step is carefully planned and executed by our expert team. Pre-manufactured plunge and garden pools mean a faster, cleaner process with fewer surprises. You will be informed and involved throughout.

Initial Consultation

Duration: 1-2 hours

We visit your home to assess your backyard, understand your vision, and discuss possibilities. You will receive a detailed proposal within 48 hours.

  • Free on-site assessment
  • Property measurements
  • Budget discussion
  • Pool options review

Design & Pool Selection

Duration: 1-2 weeks

Choose from our selection of premium Latham and Thursday Pools fiberglass models. Select your color, size, and features with our expert guidance.

  • Pool shape selection
  • Gelcoat color options
  • Feature additions
  • Final contract signing

Permits & Preparation

Duration: 2-4 weeks

We handle all permit applications with your local municipality. Utility locations are marked and your pool is ordered from the manufacturer.

  • Permit applications
  • Utility marking (811)
  • Pool shell ordering
  • Equipment ordering

Excavation

Duration: 1-2 days

Our excavation team digs the hole to exact manufacturer specifications for your pool model. Soil conditions are assessed and adjustments made as needed.

  • Precise excavation
  • Gravel base preparation
  • Soil disposal
  • Level verification

Pool Delivery & Crane Set

Duration: 1 day

Your pre-manufactured fiberglass pool shell arrives ready to install and is carefully lowered into place by crane. Because plunge and garden pools are factory-built to exacting specs, there is no on-site forming or curing -- your pool takes shape in a single day.

  • Pool shell delivery
  • Crane positioning
  • Level adjustment
  • Initial water fill begins

Plumbing & Electrical

Duration: 2-3 days

All plumbing lines are connected and electrical work completed. Your pump, filter, heater, and any automation systems are professionally installed.

  • Filtration system
  • Pool pump installation
  • Heater connection
  • Electrical inspection

Backfill & Decking

Duration: 3-5 days

The pool is backfilled and compacted. Your chosen decking material is installed, creating the finished look of your new outdoor oasis.

  • Gravel backfill
  • Compaction
  • Deck installation
  • Coping installation

Final Inspection & Training

Duration: 1 day

Final inspections are completed, water chemistry is balanced, and we provide comprehensive training on operating and maintaining your new pool.

  • Final inspection
  • Water chemistry balance
  • Equipment training
  • Warranty documentation
