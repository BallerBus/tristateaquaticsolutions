Leadership

Company Leadership

Our leadership team brings decades of combined experience in pool installation, construction management, and customer service.

Brandon Calloway

Founder & President

Brandon brings over a decade of corporate leadership to Tri-State Aquatic Solutions, with experience as a Project Manager at JPMorgan and IT Business Partner at DuPont. He holds a Masters in Finance from the University of Delaware and a BA in Marketing from Goldey-Beacom College, combining financial acumen with a passion for building something of his own.

Before launching Tri-State Aquatic Solutions, Brandon built Pool Cleaning Dude into a thriving service operation generating $19K per month, proving his ability to scale and deliver in the pool industry. He is personally involved in every project, ensuring the same level of precision and accountability he brought to Fortune 500 companies now serves homeowners across the Main Line and Northern Delaware.

PMP Certified (Since 2014) Masters in Finance - UDel 10+ Years Corporate Leadership

Bryce

Pool Operations Director

Bryce is an experienced pool industry professional with deep expertise in pool installation, maintenance, and equipment systems. As Pool Operations Director, he oversees all field operations and ensures every installation meets Tri-State Aquatic Solutions' exacting standards.

A hands-on leader who is equally comfortable managing project timelines and working alongside the crew, Bryce brings real-world pool knowledge to every job site. His attention to detail and commitment to getting things right the first time are central to the quality our customers experience.

Pool Installation Expert Equipment Specialist Field Operations Leader
