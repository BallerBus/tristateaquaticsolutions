Company Overview

Founded by pool service professionals who transitioned into installation, we specialize in fiberglass and concrete pools for homeowners in Main Line PA and Northern Delaware. Service Model Differences

The Local Builder Approach

At Tri-State Aquatic Solutions, our service model centers on personal relationships. When you work with us:

  • You'll meet with company principals, not just salespeople
  • The same team manages your project from consultation through completion
  • We're available for questions and concerns throughout the process
  • After installation, we're still your local resource for maintenance and support
  • We live and work in the communities we serve

The National Chain Approach

Anthony & Sylvan operates with a different model that works well for many homeowners:

  • Established brand with decades of recognition
  • Standardized processes and procedures
  • Dedicated sales team, design team, and construction crews
  • Corporate-backed warranties and guarantees
  • Access to corporate resources and financing programs

What This Means for You

Neither model is inherently better. Some homeowners prefer the personal touch of a local builder where they know exactly who's responsible. Others feel more comfortable with the structure and brand backing of a national company. Consider what matters most to you.

Pricing Considerations

We won't pretend to know exactly what Anthony & Sylvan charges - their pricing varies by project just like ours. Here's what we can tell you about cost factors:

What Affects Pool Pricing

  • Pool type: Fiberglass typically costs $65,000-$95,000; concrete runs $85,000-$130,000+
  • Size and features: Larger pools with more features cost more
  • Site conditions: Rocky soil, access challenges, and grading needs impact price
  • Decking and landscaping: These can add significantly to total project cost
  • Equipment quality: Premium pumps, heaters, and automation add cost but value

Our Pricing Philosophy

We provide detailed, transparent quotes that break down every element. We don't believe in "starting at" prices that balloon with add-ons. Our initial quote includes everything we discussed - no surprises.

Getting Comparable Quotes

When comparing quotes from different builders, make sure you're comparing apples to apples:

  • Same pool dimensions and depth
  • Equivalent equipment brands and quality
  • Same decking square footage and materials
  • Equivalent features (lighting, water features, etc.)
  • Clear scope on electrical work and permits

Local Expertise: Why It Matters

Main Line Philadelphia isn't like other markets. Our region has unique characteristics that affect pool installation:

Township Variations

Lower Merion, Radnor, Tredyffrin, and other Main Line townships each have their own permit processes, setback requirements, and approval timelines. We've built relationships with local officials and understand each township's specific requirements.

Property Characteristics

Main Line properties often feature:

  • Rocky soil conditions requiring specialized excavation
  • Mature trees and established landscaping to protect
  • Historic homes with architectural considerations
  • Challenging access through narrow driveways or tight spaces
  • High water tables in certain areas

Community Standards

Many Main Line neighborhoods have HOAs or unwritten aesthetic standards. A pool that looks out of place can affect property values and neighbor relations. We understand the character of communities like Gladwyne, Villanova, Bryn Mawr, and Wayne, and design pools that complement their surroundings.
