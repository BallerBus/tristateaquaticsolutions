Latham vs Thursday Pools: Fiberglass Manufacturer Comparison

Comparing two leading fiberglass pool manufacturers. We examine quality, warranty coverage, design options, and which brand might be the best fit for your Main Line or Delaware pool project.

When shopping for a fiberglass pool, you'll encounter various manufacturers - each claiming to offer the best quality and value. Latham and Thursday Pools are two of the most respected names in the industry, each with distinct strengths.

As certified dealers who install pools from multiple manufacturers, we're providing an unbiased comparison to help you understand the differences and make an informed choice.

Thursday Pools

Design Options

Latham Design Range

  • Number of models: 90+ across all brands
  • Shapes: Rectangle, freeform, kidney, roman, grecian, oval
  • Sizes: 10' plunge pools to 40'+ family pools
  • Features: Tanning ledges, bench seating, built-in spas, beach entries
  • Colors: 10-15 gelcoat colors per line

Thursday Pools Design Range

  • Number of models: ~30 distinctive designs
  • Shapes: Rectangle, freeform, cocktail, specialty shapes
  • Sizes: 10' cocktail pools to 42' family pools
  • Features: Signature tanning ledges, unique entry designs, spa options
  • Colors: 8-10 curated gelcoat colors

Design Philosophy Differences

Latham's approach: Extensive variety to cover every preference. If you can imagine a fiberglass pool design, Latham likely offers something close. Their multiple brands (Latham, Narellan, Trilogy) each have distinct aesthetic identities.

Thursday Pools' approach: Curated collection of thoughtfully-designed pools. Each model is distinctive with unique features. Fewer options, but each is carefully crafted. Known for innovative details like the Sandal Beach entry.

Warranty Details

Latham's warranty is well-established and backed by the largest company in the industry. Their size means they'll be around to honor warranties for decades to come.

Thursday Pools' warranty includes a longer gelcoat coverage (15 years vs 10 years). This reflects their confidence in their surface quality and manufacturing consistency.

Both Warranties Are Excellent

Either manufacturer's warranty provides strong protection. The most important warranty factor is that your installer is a certified dealer - without proper installation, neither warranty will apply.

Surface Warranty: Thursday (15 years vs 10)

Dealer Networks

Latham Dealer Network

  • Largest dealer network in North America
  • Multiple dealers in most markets
  • Extensive training and certification program
  • Parts and warranty support widely available

Thursday Pools Dealer Network

Dealer Quality Matters Most

A mediocre dealer with a great brand will produce worse results than an excellent dealer with any reputable brand. When choosing between manufacturers, evaluate the local dealer's reputation, experience, and portfolio as much as the brand itself.

Making Your Decision

Choose Latham If:

  • You want maximum design variety to choose from
  • A specific Latham/Narellan/Trilogy model catches your eye
  • You value the backing of the industry's largest manufacturer
  • Multiple local dealers give you installation options
  • You prefer a "safe choice" from the established leader

Choose Thursday Pools If:

  • You want a distinctive, unique pool design
  • Their specific models (like Sandal Beach entry) appeal to you
  • The longer gelcoat warranty matters to you
  • You appreciate boutique manufacturing and direct oversight
  • A local Thursday Pools dealer has an excellent reputation

Our Recommendation

  1. Which specific pool design you love - find a shape that excites you
  2. Your local dealer's quality - installation matters more than brand
  3. Personal preference - some prefer big brands, others boutique

As certified dealers for both manufacturers, we recommend based on your specific preferences - not which brand we prefer to install. Both will serve you well for decades.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Latham or Thursday Pools better quality?

Both manufacturers produce high-quality fiberglass pools. Latham is the industry's largest manufacturer with the most design variety and extensive dealer network. Thursday Pools is known for innovative designs and excellent customer service. Quality is comparable - the best choice often depends on which designs you prefer.

What warranty do Latham and Thursday Pools offer?

Latham offers a lifetime structural warranty and 10-year surface warranty on most models. Thursday Pools offers a lifetime structural warranty and 15-year gelcoat warranty. Both warranties require installation by a certified dealer and proper maintenance. The warranties are among the strongest in the industry.

Which manufacturer has more pool designs?

Latham offers approximately 90+ fiberglass pool models across multiple brands (Latham, Narellan, Trilogy), making it the largest selection in the industry. Thursday Pools offers around 30 models but focuses on distinctive, thoughtfully-designed shapes. Both include various sizes, shapes, and built-in features.

Are Thursday Pools more expensive than Latham?

Prices are generally comparable for similar-sized pools. Thursday Pools' unique designs may command slight premiums. Latham's high volume allows competitive pricing across their range. Final pricing depends more on the specific model, features, and local dealer than the manufacturer brand.

Which fiberglass pool brand does Tri-State Aquatic install?

As certified dealers, we install pools from multiple manufacturers including both Latham and Thursday Pools. We recommend specific brands based on your design preferences, property requirements, and budget. We're not locked into one manufacturer - we choose the best pool for each customer.
