FAQ

Common Questions

How much does pool automation cost?

Pool automation systems range from $1,500-$3,000 for basic systems to $3,500-$5,000+ for full-featured controllers with multiple expansion panels. Installation adds $500-$1,500 depending on system complexity, equipment compatibility, and wiring requirements.

What can pool automation control?

Modern pool automation can control pumps, variable speed settings, filters, heaters, LED lights, waterfalls, fountains, salt chlorine generators, chemical feeders, automatic covers, and spillover spas. Everything is manageable from a smartphone app, touchscreen panel, or voice assistant.

Which pool automation brand is best?

Pentair, Hayward, and Jandy all make excellent automation systems. The best choice depends on your existing equipment (matching brands provides the best integration), desired features, and budget. We can assess your setup and recommend the optimal system.

Can I retrofit automation to my existing pool?

Yes, automation can be added to most existing pools. The system connects to your current pump, heater, lights, and other equipment. Some older equipment may need relay interfaces or upgrades for full compatibility. We evaluate your setup during the consultation.

Does pool automation work with Alexa and Google Home?

Yes, all major pool automation brands integrate with Amazon Alexa and most support Google Home as well. You can use voice commands to turn on lights, adjust temperature, activate water features, and more. Setup is typically straightforward through the manufacturer's app.
