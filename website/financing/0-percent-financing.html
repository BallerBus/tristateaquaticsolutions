How Promotional Financing Works

Promotional financing offers allow you to finance your pool with reduced or no interest for a set period. However, it is important to understand the different types of promotional offers to avoid surprises.

The key is understanding whether your promotion is "Same-As-Cash" or "Deferred Interest" - they work very differently.

Same-As-Cash (Recommended)

If you pay off the entire balance before the promotional period ends, you pay zero interest. If you do not pay it off in time, interest is only charged on the remaining balance going forward. Our preferred financing partners offer this type.

Deferred Interest (Use Caution)

If you do not pay off the balance before the promotional period ends, interest is charged retroactively from the original purchase date on the full original balance. This can result in a large surprise charge. Always ask which type you are being offered.

Important: Know Your Offer Type

Always confirm whether your promotional offer is "Same-As-Cash" or "Deferred Interest" before signing. Our financing partners primarily offer Same-As-Cash promotions, but always verify the terms in your loan documents.

Questions

Promotional Financing FAQ

How does 0% promotional financing work?

0% promotional financing offers allow you to finance your pool with no interest charges during a promotional period (typically 12-24 months). You make equal monthly payments during this time. If the balance is paid off before the promotional period ends, you pay no interest.

What happens after the promotional period ends?

There are two types of promotional offers. Same-As-Cash promotions mean if you pay off the balance before the period ends, you pay no interest. Deferred interest means if you do not pay off the balance in full, interest is charged retroactively from the purchase date. Always check which type you are offered.

What credit score do I need for 0% financing?

Promotional financing typically requires good to excellent credit. Most 0% offers require a credit score of 720 or higher. The best promotional offers (longer terms, higher amounts) usually require scores of 740+.

Can I make extra payments during the promotional period?

Yes, and you should! Making extra payments helps ensure you pay off the balance before the promotional period ends. There are no prepayment penalties, so you can pay as much as you want each month.

What if I cannot pay off the balance in time?

With Same-As-Cash promotions (which our partners offer), you will start paying interest only on the remaining balance at the standard rate. Plan your payments to pay off as much as possible during the 0% period, and the remaining balance will be much smaller.
