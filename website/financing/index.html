Why Finance Your Pool?

A backyard pool is more than a luxury - it is an investment in your home's value, your family's well-being, and years of memories to come. With today's financing options, you can enjoy your pool now while spreading the cost over time.

Many homeowners find that their monthly pool payment is less than what they would spend on family vacations, gym memberships, or entertainment. The difference? Your pool is always there, adding value to your home and providing a private retreat just steps away.

  • Keep cash reserves for emergencies and other investments
  • Enjoy your pool immediately rather than saving for years
  • Fixed monthly payments make budgeting easy
  • Pool adds 5-15% to home value in our market
  • Interest may be tax-deductible (consult your tax advisor)

Options

Types of Pool Financing

Multiple financing options let you choose the approach that works best for your situation.

Home Equity Loan

Borrow against your home's equity with fixed rates and predictable payments.

  • Typical APR 5-8%
  • Terms 5-30 years
  • Approval Time 2-4 weeks
  • Best For Lowest rates

Pool Loan (Unsecured)

Dedicated pool financing without using your home as collateral.

  • Typical APR 6-12%
  • Terms 5-20 years
  • Approval Time 24-48 hours
  • Best For Quick approval

Personal Loan

Flexible personal loans from banks and credit unions for smaller projects.

  • Typical APR 8-15%
  • Terms 2-7 years
  • Approval Time Same day
  • Best For Smaller amounts

Questions

Financing FAQ

What credit score do I need to finance a pool?

Most pool financing options require a credit score of 640 or higher for the best rates. However, options are available for scores as low as 580. Higher credit scores typically qualify for better interest rates and terms.

Can I pay off my pool loan early?

Yes, most pool financing options allow early payoff without prepayment penalties. This can save you significant interest over the life of the loan. Check specific loan terms during the application process.

How long does financing approval take?

Many applicants receive a decision within 24-48 hours for unsecured pool loans and personal loans. Home equity loans and HELOCs may take 2-4 weeks due to property appraisal requirements.

Is financing available for pool renovations?

Yes, our financing partners offer loans for both new pool installations and pool renovations. The same terms and options apply to renovation projects over $15,000.

Do I need to put money down?

Most unsecured pool loans do not require a down payment. However, some projects may benefit from a down payment to reduce monthly payments or secure better rates. We can discuss options during your consultation.
