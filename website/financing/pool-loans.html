Questions

Pool Loan FAQ

What credit score do I need for a pool loan?

Most pool loan lenders require a minimum credit score of 640 for approval. Scores of 680+ typically qualify for the best rates (starting around 6.99% APR). Some lenders offer options for scores as low as 580, though rates will be higher.

How long does pool loan approval take?

One of the biggest advantages of unsecured pool loans is speed. Most applicants receive a decision within 24-48 hours. Funding can happen within a few days of approval, allowing your pool project to begin quickly.

Can I pay off my pool loan early?

Yes, most pool financing partners allow early payoff without prepayment penalties. This means you can pay off your loan faster if your financial situation changes, saving money on interest.

What documents do I need to apply?

For most pool loans, you will need proof of income (recent pay stubs or tax returns), valid identification, and your pool project quote. Some lenders may request additional documentation during underwriting.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Most lenders use a "soft pull" for pre-qualification, which does not affect your credit score. A "hard pull" typically happens only after you accept a loan offer and move forward with the full application.

How is the money disbursed?

Funds are typically disbursed in stages as your pool project progresses. This protects both you and the lender by ensuring work is completed before full payment is made to the contractor.
