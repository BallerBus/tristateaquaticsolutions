Understanding Your Options

Key factors to consider before making your decision

Shape Requirements

If you need an unusual shape, very deep diving area, or exact custom dimensions, concrete offers more flexibility. But fiberglass offers 100+ shapes that fit most backyards beautifully.

Timeline Pressure

Planning a summer pool party? Fiberglass can be installed and ready in weeks. Concrete requires patience - spring installations often aren't complete until late summer.

True Cost of Ownership

Concrete may seem similar in upfront cost, but factor in resurfacing ($10-15K every 10-15 years), higher chemical costs, and acid washing - fiberglass saves significantly long-term.
