Common Questions

Greenville Pool FAQ

Can you work with our estate's existing design team?

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with landscape architects, estate managers, and design teams on Chateau Country properties. We can integrate seamlessly into your existing project team or work directly with you.

Do you have experience with historic property considerations?

Yes. We have worked on properties with historic designations and understand the importance of sympathetic design that enhances rather than detracts from historic character. We can coordinate with preservation authorities when needed.

How do you handle privacy concerns during construction?

We understand that Greenville property owners value their privacy. Our crews are professional and discreet, we use unmarked vehicles when requested, and we can work with your estate staff to coordinate access and scheduling.

What is the typical budget for a Greenville estate pool?

Most Greenville concrete pool projects range from $100,000 to $200,000+, depending on size, features, and finishes. Estate-scale infinity pools with integrated features can exceed $200,000. We provide detailed proposals with transparent pricing.

How long does an estate pool project take?

Custom concrete pools for estate properties typically take 12-16 weeks from groundbreaking to completion. Complex projects with extensive hardscaping or coordination requirements may take longer. We provide detailed timelines in our proposals.
