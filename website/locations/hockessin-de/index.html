Common Questions

Hockessin Pool FAQ

How fast can you respond to a service call in Hockessin?

As a Hockessin-based company, we can typically respond to urgent issues the same day. Our office is just minutes from most Hockessin neighborhoods. For routine questions, we respond within 24 business hours.

Do you have experience with Hockessin HOAs?

Yes, we have worked with many of the HOAs in Hockessin and surrounding areas. We can provide the architectural drawings, material samples, and documentation your HOA requires for approval.

What permits are required for a pool in Hockessin?

Hockessin is part of New Castle County, so you will need a building permit from the county. Delaware also requires a barrier (fence) around the pool. We handle all permitting and inspections for you. For detailed permit requirements, see our New Castle County Pool Permit Guide.

How does Hockessin's terrain affect pool installation?

Hockessin's rolling terrain and clay-heavy soil can present challenges, but we have been building pools here for years. We know exactly how to handle grading, drainage, and soil stabilization for local conditions.

What is the typical timeline for a Hockessin pool project?

Fiberglass pools are typically installed in 2-4 weeks from permit approval. Concrete pools take 8-12 weeks. Being local means we can often start sooner and complete faster than competitors coming from out of the area.
