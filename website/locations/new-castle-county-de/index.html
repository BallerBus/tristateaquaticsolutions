Common Questions

New Castle County Pool FAQ

How long does pool installation take in New Castle County?

Fiberglass pools are typically installed in 2-4 weeks from permit approval. Concrete pools take 8-12 weeks. Delaware's streamlined permit process often means faster start times than neighboring Pennsylvania.

Do I need a permit for a pool in New Castle County?

Yes, all in-ground pools require a building permit from New Castle County. We handle the entire permit process for you, including application, plans, and coordination with inspectors.

What are the fence requirements for pools in Delaware?

Delaware requires a barrier (fence) of at least 48 inches surrounding the pool with self-closing, self-latching gates. We include barrier consultation and can coordinate fence installation as part of your project.

How much can I save with Delaware's no sales tax?

Delaware has no sales tax, which can save you thousands on pool materials. On a typical $75,000 pool project, this can represent $4,500-$6,000 in savings compared to a pool built in Pennsylvania (6% sales tax).

Can you work with my HOA on pool approval?

Absolutely. We have experience working with HOAs throughout New Castle County. We can provide architectural drawings, material samples, and documentation to support your HOA approval process.
