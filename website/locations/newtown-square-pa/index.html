FAQ

Newtown Square Pool Questions

How long does pool installation take?

Fiberglass pools typically install in 2-4 weeks. Concrete pools take 8-12 weeks. We coordinate all permits for efficient timelines.

Does my HOA need to approve the pool?

Most Newtown Square HOAs require approval. We review your HOA guidelines and help prepare the necessary documentation for approval.

What size pool fits a typical lot here?

Newtown Square lots vary widely. We assess your property's size, setbacks, and impervious coverage limits to recommend appropriate options.

Can you match existing patio materials?

Absolutely. We work with your existing hardscape or design new patios that complement your home and landscaping.

What maintenance is included?

Every Newtown Square installation includes 1-2 years of complimentary maintenance service. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages.
