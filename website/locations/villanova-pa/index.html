FAQ

Villanova Pool Questions

How long does pool installation take in Villanova?

Fiberglass pools typically install in 2-4 weeks. Concrete pools take 8-12 weeks. We coordinate all Radnor Township permits to ensure a smooth timeline.

Do you handle permits for Radnor Township?

Yes. We manage the entire permitting process with Radnor Township, including zoning applications, safety barrier requirements, and final inspections.

What pool type is best for a Villanova estate?

For larger properties and custom designs, concrete offers maximum flexibility. Fiberglass provides faster installation with excellent durability for most residential needs.

Do you offer pool maintenance services?

Yes. Every Villanova installation includes 1-2 years of complimentary maintenance. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages.

Can you work with existing landscape design?

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with landscape architects and designers to ensure seamless integration of pool, hardscape, and plantings.
