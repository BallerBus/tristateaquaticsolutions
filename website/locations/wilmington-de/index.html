Common Questions

Wilmington Pool FAQ

Do you serve all Wilmington neighborhoods?

Yes, we serve all neighborhoods in Wilmington and the surrounding area, including North Wilmington, Brandywine, Wawaset Park, Trolley Square, Alapocas, and beyond. Being based in nearby Hockessin means we can respond quickly to any location in the city.

Can you work with smaller urban lots?

Absolutely. We have experience with pools of all sizes, including plunge pools and space-efficient designs that work beautifully on smaller city lots. Our design team can help you maximize your available space while creating a stunning backyard oasis.

What about historic district requirements?

We have experience working in Wilmington's historic districts and understand the additional review processes that may be required. We design pools that respect the character of historic neighborhoods while still delivering modern functionality and enjoyment.

How long does installation take in Wilmington?

Fiberglass pools are typically installed in 2-4 weeks from permit approval. Concrete pools take 8-12 weeks. Delaware's streamlined permit process and our proximity to Wilmington help ensure quick project starts.

What is the sales tax situation for Wilmington residents?

Great news - Delaware has no sales tax, which means significant savings on your pool materials. This can represent thousands of dollars in savings compared to having the same pool installed in a neighboring state like Pennsylvania.
