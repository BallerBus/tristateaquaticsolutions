Understanding Rockland

Historic Character, Modern Pools

Rockland's transformation from a 19th-century mill community to an upscale residential area has created a neighborhood with unique character. Historic stone buildings, mature trees, and the proximity to the Brandywine Creek give the area a distinctive charm.

Pool design in Rockland should complement this historic context. We often recommend natural stone coping, classic shapes, and materials that blend with the existing architecture. However, we also create sleek, modern designs for the neighborhood's newer homes.

The varied terrain along the Brandywine presents opportunities for creative pool placement. Elevated pools with valley views, terraced designs that work with slopes, and naturalistic pools that echo the nearby creek are all possibilities we've explored with Rockland homeowners.
