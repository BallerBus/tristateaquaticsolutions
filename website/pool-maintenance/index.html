FAQ

Common Questions

How often should I have my pool professionally serviced?

For optimal water quality and equipment longevity, we recommend weekly professional service during swimming season. This ensures consistent chemical balance, clean water, and early detection of any equipment issues before they become costly repairs.

What's included in your weekly maintenance service?

Our weekly service includes skimming debris from the surface, vacuuming the pool floor, brushing walls and tiles, testing and balancing water chemistry, emptying skimmer and pump baskets, inspecting equipment operation, and providing a detailed service report after each visit.

When should I schedule pool opening and closing?

In the PA/DE region, we recommend opening your pool in late April to early May when temperatures consistently stay above 60 degrees, and closing in late September to mid-October before the first hard freeze. Booking early is important as these are our busiest times of year.

Do you service pools you didn't install?

Absolutely! We service all types of inground pools regardless of who installed them. Our maintenance expertise covers fiberglass, concrete, and vinyl liner pools from any builder. Many of our maintenance customers later become installation customers when they are ready for upgrades or new pools.
