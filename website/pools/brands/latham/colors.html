Color Technology

Why Latham Colors Last

Every Latham color is engineered with advanced technology for decades of vibrant beauty.

UV Resistant

Advanced UV stabilizers prevent color fading from intense sunlight exposure, keeping your pool vibrant year after year.

Chemical Resistant

Engineered to withstand pool chemicals without discoloration, staining, or surface degradation over time.

Blister Resistant

Crystite technology prevents osmotic blistering that can affect lesser gelcoat finishes, ensuring a smooth surface.
