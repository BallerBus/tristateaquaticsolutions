Advanced Technology

Why Latham Leads the Industry

Latham's proprietary manufacturing technology creates pools that are stronger, more beautiful, and longer-lasting than the competition.

Advanced Composite Technology

Carbon fiber and biaxial reinforcements combined with ceramic-infused polyester increase shell strength by 32% compared to standard fiberglass.

Crystite Color Technology

Industry-leading gelcoat finishes engineered for UV, blister, and chemical resistance. Colors stay vibrant and fade-resistant for decades.

Reinforced Ribbing

Strategic ribbing patterns provide maximum structural integrity and resistance to ground movement, freeze-thaw cycles, and hydrostatic pressure.
