Latham Pool Warranty

A Latham pool is not just built to last - it is backed to last. Their limited lifetime warranty delivers peace of mind that your investment is protected for years to come.

Structural Protection

Structural Warranty

Lifetime

Latham warrants the pool against leaking due to structural failure for as long as the original purchaser owns the pool.

  • Water loss due to fracture or crack in the pool shell
  • Voids or separations arising from manufacturing defects
  • Defects in workmanship or materials
  • Residential applications only
Surface Protection

Surface Warranty

Lifetime*

The surface finish (gelcoat) is warranted against osmotic blistering due to defects in materials or workmanship.

  • Osmotic blistering protection
  • Crystite and Diamond Series: Lifetime coverage
  • UV and fade resistance
  • Accelerated weather testing to ASTM standards

Requirements

Warranty Conditions

To maintain warranty coverage, certain conditions must be met.

Licensed Installation

Pool must be installed by a licensed contractor who complies with all applicable state and local building codes and ANSI/APSP-5 standards.

Proper Maintenance

Owner must follow the Manufacturer's Use & Care Manual and maintain proper water chemistry according to guidelines.

Residential Use Only

Warranty applies to residential applications only. Commercial or rental property installations are not covered.

Warranty Registration

Pool should be registered with Latham through the warranty registration process for coverage verification.

Important Information

What Is Not Covered

Understanding exclusions helps set proper expectations for warranty coverage.

  • Commercial or non-residential applications
  • Pools sold as "blemished" or "as is"
  • Damage caused by improper installation, storage, or transportation
  • Damage from improper water chemistry or maintenance
  • Normal wear and tear or cosmetic issues
  • Damage from acts of nature, vandalism, or misuse
  • Installation workmanship (covered by installer, not Latham)

Note: Latham pools are sold and installed by independent dealers. Latham does not warrant installation workmanship. Your installer provides their own installation warranty. Tri-State Aquatic Solutions provides comprehensive installation coverage as part of our service.
