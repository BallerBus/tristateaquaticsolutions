Innovation Leader

Why Thursday Pools Stands Out

Thursday Pools pioneered features that have become industry standards, always focused on enhancing the swimming experience.

Patented Beach Entry

The world's first beach entry fiberglass pool design (US Patent 10,472,839). Zero-entry transitions bring the shoreline experience to your backyard.

Built-in Tanning Ledges

Integrated sun shelves at the perfect 12" depth for loungers. Relax in shallow water while catching rays - a resort experience at home.

Infinity Edge Ready

Select models are designed for infinity edge installation, creating stunning visual effects with water seemingly flowing to the horizon.

Swim Jet Compatible

Many Thursday models can be equipped with Badu Swim Jet Systems for endless lap swimming and resistance exercise in a compact space.

Family-Focused Design

Every pool is designed with families in mind - safe entry points, comfortable seating, kid-friendly depths, and spaces for conversation.

Multi-Layer Construction

Premium gelcoat, vinyl ester barrier coat, and hand-laid fiberglass layers create maximum strength and durability in every pool.
