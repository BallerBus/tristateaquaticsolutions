Structural Protection

Structural Warranty

Lifetime

Thursday Pools warrants your pool against leaking due to structural failures caused by workmanship and materials used in manufacturing.

  • Coverage for manufacturing workmanship issues
  • Material defect protection
  • Original owner coverage
Surface Protection

Surface Warranty

  • Osmotic blistering protection
  • Premium gelcoat coverage
  • UV and chemical resistance
  • Fade resistance included
Built to Last

Multi-Layer Construction

1

Premium Gelcoat

Smooth, non-porous top layer that resists algae, scratches, and fading while providing a luxurious finish.

2

Warranty Benefits

Your Protection

Understanding your warranty coverage helps protect your pool investment.

Original Owner

Warranty coverage extends to the original retail purchaser for residential applications only.

Documentation

Keep your purchase documentation and warranty registration for easy claims processing.

Proper Maintenance

Following manufacturer guidelines for water chemistry and care maintains warranty validity.

Important Information

What Is Not Covered

Understanding exclusions helps set proper expectations for warranty coverage.

  • Commercial or non-residential pool applications
  • Damage caused by improper installation practices
  • Improper water chemistry or failure to maintain proper balance
  • Normal wear and cosmetic issues that do not affect function
  • Damage from acts of nature, accidents, or misuse
  • Installation workmanship (covered by your installer separately)

Note: Thursday Pools warrants manufacturing quality. Installation is performed by independent dealers who provide their own workmanship warranty. Tri-State Aquatic Solutions provides comprehensive installation coverage as part of our service.
