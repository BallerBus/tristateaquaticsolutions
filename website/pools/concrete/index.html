Questions Answered

Concrete Pool FAQ

Everything you need to know about investing in a custom concrete pool.

A custom concrete pool typically takes 8-12 weeks from groundbreaking to completion. This timeline includes excavation, steel reinforcement installation, gunite application, proper curing time (critical for structural integrity), plaster finishing, equipment installation, and final detailing. While longer than fiberglass, this timeline ensures a pool built to last generations with full customization to your exact specifications.
Our concrete pools include premium features as standard that other builders charge $20,000+ extra for: automatic safety cover, ORP/pH automated water treatment, high-efficiency variable speed pump, premium plaster or pebble finish, LED lighting, waterline tile, and comprehensive warranty coverage. Integrated spa with spillover is available as an option for the ultimate luxury experience.
Concrete pool installations in Pennsylvania typically range from $80,000 to $130,000, depending on size, design complexity, and features selected. This includes the automatic cover and ORP/pH system that other builders charge $20,000+ extra for. Premium finishes, custom water features, integrated spas, and elaborate hardscaping can increase the investment. We provide detailed proposals during your consultation.
Concrete pools offer unlimited design flexibility - any shape, size, depth, or feature you can imagine. They allow for truly integrated spas, beach entries, infinity edges, grottos, and custom water features that fiberglass cannot accommodate. Concrete also provides the longest lifespan (50+ years), highest property value addition, and is ideal for estate-scale properties, architecturally significant homes, or anyone who wants a truly one-of-a-kind pool.
Our concrete pools include the longest warranty in the industry: lifetime structural warranty on the gunite shell, 10-year warranty on plaster finish, 5-year warranty on plumbing and equipment, plus manufacturer warranties on the automatic cover and ORP/pH system. Our comprehensive warranty package reflects our confidence in the quality of materials and craftsmanship we deliver.
