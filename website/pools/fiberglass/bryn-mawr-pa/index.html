Multi-Township Expertise

Bryn Mawr Permit Navigation

Bryn Mawr's unique geography spans three townships - Lower Merion in Montgomery County, plus Haverford and Radnor in Delaware County. Each has distinct permit requirements, and properties near boundaries may need coordination across jurisdictions.

Our team has extensive experience with all three townships' building departments, ensuring your project meets every requirement regardless of which jurisdiction your property falls under.

  • Lower Merion Township permit coordination (Montgomery County)
  • Haverford Township applications (Delaware County)
  • Radnor Township compliance verification
  • Historic district considerations where applicable
  • Site plans respecting mature landscaping and trees
