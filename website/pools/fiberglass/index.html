Why Fiberglass

The Smart Choice for Modern Pool Ownership

Today's fiberglass technology delivers a premium pool experience with intelligent engineering that saves you time, money, and effort for decades to come.

Lightning-Fast Installation

While concrete pools take 3-6 months, your fiberglass pool is installed in just 2-4 weeks. The factory-finished shell arrives ready to set, meaning less disruption to your life and faster enjoyment of your investment.

Virtually No Maintenance

The smooth, non-porous gelcoat surface naturally resists algae growth. No acid washing. No replastering. No endless scrubbing. Just more time enjoying your pool and less time maintaining it.

Gentle on Skin

Unlike rough concrete surfaces that can scrape feet and irritate skin, the silky-smooth gelcoat finish feels luxurious against your skin. Perfect for families with young children who love to play in the water.

Built to Last

Modern fiberglass pools are engineered to flex with ground movement and withstand freeze-thaw cycles. Your investment is protected by a lifetime structural warranty and 15-year surface warranty.

Lower Lifetime Costs

Reduced chemical requirements, lower heating costs, and no resurfacing needs mean fiberglass pools cost 50% less to maintain over their lifetime. Your savings start from day one and compound over decades.

Premium Features Included

Tanning ledges, built-in benches, designer steps, and integrated spa spillovers come as standard features in many models. What would be expensive add-ons with concrete are elegantly molded into your fiberglass pool.

Questions Answered

Fiberglass Pool FAQ

Get answers to the most common questions about fiberglass pool installation.

Most fiberglass pool installations are completed in 2-4 weeks from groundbreaking to first swim. This is significantly faster than concrete pools, which typically require 3-6 months. The speed comes from the factory-finished shell that arrives ready to install, eliminating weeks of on-site curing and finishing work.
Fiberglass pool installations in Pennsylvania typically range from $60,000 to $100,000, depending on size, features, and site conditions. This includes the pool, equipment, basic decking, and installation. Premium features like tanning ledges, LED lighting, automation systems, and upgraded hardscaping add to the base price. We provide detailed, itemized quotes during your free consultation.
Yes, modern fiberglass pools are engineered specifically to handle Pennsylvania's freeze-thaw cycles. The flexible gelcoat surface expands and contracts with temperature changes, unlike rigid concrete that can crack. The fiberglass shell is also resistant to ground movement and hydrostatic pressure. With proper winterization and a quality cover, your fiberglass pool will provide decades of trouble-free service through our Northeastern winters.
Fiberglass pools require approximately 50% less maintenance than concrete pools. The smooth, non-porous gelcoat surface naturally resists algae growth and does not require acid washing or replastering. Basic care includes regular skimming, maintaining proper water chemistry, running the filter system, and occasional brushing. We include one year of complimentary maintenance service with every installation to help you learn the routine.
While fiberglass pools come in pre-designed shapes, there are over 100 models available in various sizes (from 10 to 40+ feet), depths, and configurations. Many models include built-in tanning ledges, benches, steps, and spillover spa attachments. You can select from 50+ gelcoat colors and finishes. Beyond the pool itself, you have complete customization of the surrounding hardscape, lighting, water features, and automation systems.
Our fiberglass pools include comprehensive warranty coverage: a lifetime structural warranty on the shell that covers cracking, blistering, and structural defects; a 15-year surface warranty on the gelcoat finish; and 3-5 year warranties on pumps, filters, and other equipment from their manufacturers. Tri-State Aquatic Solutions also provides our own installation warranty for added peace of mind.
Fiberglass pools offer faster installation (2-4 weeks vs 3-6 months), significantly lower lifetime maintenance (no replastering, acid washing, or constant algae scrubbing), and lower chemical costs due to the non-porous surface. Concrete pools offer unlimited shape customization for truly unique or estate-scale designs, but at higher cost and longer timelines. For most residential applications where a pre-designed shape works, fiberglass provides the best combination of quality, value, and long-term ownership experience.
Yes, we partner with several reputable financing providers to offer competitive rates and flexible terms for qualified buyers. Options include home equity loans, pool-specific financing programs, and personal loans. Many homeowners find that monthly payments are comparable to what they might spend on club memberships, vacations, or other entertainment expenses. We are happy to discuss financing options during your free consultation to find the best fit for your budget and financial goals.
