Lower Merion Township maintains specific permit requirements through Montgomery County for all pool installations. The township's commitment to preserving neighborhood character and property values means careful attention to permitting details.

Our team has extensive experience with Lower Merion's building department. We understand the specific requirements for each neighborhood - from historic preservation considerations in Merion Station to estate-level projects in Gladwyne requiring environmental considerations.
