Our Installation Process

1

Property Consultation

We visit your Newark property to assess the site, discuss your vision, review any HOA requirements, and identify the best pool placement for your yard and lifestyle.

2

Custom Design

Our designers create a pool plan tailored to your property, considering lot dimensions, HOA guidelines, and your family's needs. We'll present options that maximize your space and budget.

3

HOA & Permit Coordination

We handle all New Castle County permits and assist with your HOA approval process if applicable. Our experience with Newark communities helps streamline approvals.

4

Site Preparation

Careful excavation using equipment suited to your property. We protect existing landscaping and coordinate with utilities for a smooth start to construction.

5

Pool Installation

Precision placement and leveling of your fiberglass pool shell, followed by plumbing, electrical connections, and backfill. The pool itself installs in just one day!

6

Finishing & Handoff

Decking installation, equipment setup, and landscape restoration. Final inspection, water chemistry balancing, and comprehensive owner training complete your project.
