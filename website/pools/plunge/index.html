Understanding Plunge Pools

What Is a Plunge Pool?

A plunge pool is a compact, pre-fabricated pool typically measuring 10 to 16 feet in length. Unlike a traditional in-ground pool designed for lap swimming, plunge pools are built for relaxation, cooling off, light exercise, and backyard entertaining.

They are manufactured in a controlled factory environment for consistent quality, then delivered directly to your home. Because the pool shell arrives ready to install, on-site construction time is dramatically reduced compared to traditional pool building.

Plunge pools are the fastest-growing segment of the residential pool industry. Homeowners across the Main Line and Northern Delaware are choosing them for their blend of luxury, affordability, and practicality.
