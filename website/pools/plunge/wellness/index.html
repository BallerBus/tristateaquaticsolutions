The Evidence Is Clear

Science-Backed Wellness, In Your Backyard

Cold water immersion and hydrotherapy are among the most studied recovery methods in sports medicine and wellness research. Here is what the evidence consistently shows.

Cold Water Immersion

Research consistently demonstrates that brief cold water exposure (2 to 11 minutes at 50-59°F) reduces systemic inflammation, stimulates the immune system, and enhances mental clarity. Regular cold immersion is associated with increased norepinephrine levels, which play a key role in focus, attention, and mood regulation.

Hydrotherapy Jets

Targeted hydrotherapy jet systems deliver pressurized water to specific muscle groups and joints, providing relief from chronic pain, muscle tension, and post-exercise soreness. Medical literature supports warm water hydrotherapy for improving range of motion, reducing joint stiffness, and promoting relaxation through parasympathetic nervous system activation.

Contrast Therapy (Hot & Cold)

Alternating between hot and cold water exposure has been shown to improve cardiovascular function by repeatedly dilating and constricting blood vessels. Peer-reviewed research links contrast water therapy to improved circulation, faster metabolic waste removal, reduced muscle damage markers, and accelerated recovery after physical exertion.

The Daily Routine Effect

Consistent daily cold or warm water immersion is associated with measurable reductions in cortisol (the primary stress hormone), improved sleep onset and quality, and elevated baseline levels of dopamine and norepinephrine. The compounding effect of a daily wellness routine is one of the most powerful aspects of having a pool steps from your back door.

Benefits referenced above reflect general themes from published medical and sports science research on hydrotherapy and cold water immersion. Individual results vary. Consult your physician before beginning any cold exposure regimen.

Yes, Even in January

The number one question we hear: “Will I actually use it in winter?” The answer is yes — and here is why that concern disappears the moment you own one.

Insulated Shell

Multi-layer insulated pool shells retain your target temperature with minimal energy loss. Whether you are holding 45°F or 102°F, the insulation works in your favor.

Efficient Climate Control

High-efficiency heat pumps and chillers maintain your exact temperature regardless of outdoor conditions. Operating costs stay reasonable even through a Pennsylvania winter.

Smart Controls

Set your temperature from your phone before you even step outside. Schedule your pool to be ready at 5:30 AM for your morning routine. Adjust from the couch, the office, or the airport.

Cold Plunge Is FOR Cold Weather

The entire point of cold plunge is deliberate cold exposure. Winter does not make your pool less useful — it makes it more authentic. Step out of your warm home, immerse in 45°F water, and feel the difference.
