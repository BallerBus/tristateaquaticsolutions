Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Want to Know

The Brandywine measures 9 feet wide by 21 feet long, making it the largest pool in our lineup. It features a depth range of 3.5' to 5.5' and holds approximately 8,500 gallons of water. It comfortably accommodates 6 to 10 people, making it ideal for families and entertaining.
A typical Brandywine installation takes 8 to 12 weeks from contract signing to swimming. This includes permitting, excavation, pool installation, equipment setup, and final inspection. Timeline can vary based on permit processing times and site conditions in your area.
Yes. The Brandywine is available with an integrated spa section, making it a true pool-and-spa combination. The built-in spa includes dedicated hydrotherapy jets, independent temperature control, and a beautiful spillover water feature connecting pool and spa. This is the most popular upgrade for The Brandywine.
The Brandywine price of $68,000 - $85,000 includes the complete pool shell, color-changing LED lighting, variable speed pump, C-200 cartridge filtration, 6 return jets, dual surface skimmers, stainless steel handrails, built-in bench seating, and a 15-year watertight guarantee. Permits, decking, electrical, and landscaping are priced separately based on your specific project.
The Brandywine is available in three finish options: Precast Concrete for the fastest installation with consistent quality, Plaster & Tile for a fully customized look with hand-applied finishes, and Fiberglass Composite for a smooth, low-maintenance surface. Each option comes with its own color palette and your consultant can walk you through samples.
The Brandywine (9'x21') is our largest and most premium pool, ideal for entertaining and families who want the ultimate backyard experience. The Gladwyne (9'x16') is our most popular mid-size option perfect for families. The Devon (7'x13') is our compact plunge pool for wellness and smaller spaces.
