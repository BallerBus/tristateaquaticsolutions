Project Background

The clients fell in love with a charming Bryn Mawr row home but were disappointed by the small backyard. They had been told by multiple builders that their yard was simply too small for a pool.

Working long hours in Center City, they dreamed of coming home to their own private oasis - a place to cool off after work and entertain friends on weekends without the hassle of traveling to a pool or beach.

They found us through our plunge pool content and were excited to learn that a pool was not only possible but could be the perfect fit for their lifestyle and budget.

The Challenge & Our Solution

Proving that great things come in small packages

The Challenge

Fitting a functional, beautiful pool into an extremely limited space while maintaining usable yard area and complying with setback requirements.

  • Only 30x40 ft of usable backyard space
  • Required 5 ft setbacks on all sides per township code
  • Narrow access path (only 8 ft wide) for equipment
  • Underground utilities running through yard
  • Neighbors in close proximity (privacy concerns)
  • Budget-conscious clients who wanted value

Our Solution

A compact plunge pool design that maximizes every square inch while creating a surprisingly spacious feeling.

  • Selected 10x20 ft fiberglass plunge pool - perfect fit for space
  • Positioned pool to maximize remaining patio space
  • Used compact equipment package to minimize footprint
  • Coordinated utility relocation with township
  • Designed raised privacy walls with integrated planters
  • Added multi-level deck to create visual interest

Key Takeaways

Lessons for small-space pool success

Don't Accept "Too Small"

Multiple builders told these clients their yard couldn't accommodate a pool. With the right approach and product selection, almost any yard can work - it just takes creativity and experience.

Plunge Pools Pack Value

At roughly half the cost of a full-sized pool, plunge pools deliver 80% of the enjoyment. For busy professionals, the compact size means less maintenance and more time relaxing.

Features Over Footage

Built-in bench seating, jets for exercise, and LED lighting make this small pool feel luxurious. Smart feature selection beats square footage every time.

Privacy Creates Intimacy

In tight urban/suburban lots, privacy walls with integrated planters transform a small pool into a private retreat. The enclosed feeling actually enhances the experience.
