Project Background

The clients had recently completed a significant renovation of their historic Gladwyne estate and were ready to transform their expansive backyard into a world-class outdoor living space.

Having traveled extensively and experienced luxury resort pools around the world, they had a clear vision: a pool that would serve as both a stunning focal point for entertaining and a serene retreat for daily relaxation.

Their expectations were high, and they sought a builder who could deliver a premium result that would complement their meticulously maintained property and match the quality of their home's renovation.

The Challenge & Our Solution

Understanding the unique requirements and delivering beyond expectations

The Challenge

Creating a luxury pool installation that would meet the exacting standards of discerning Main Line homeowners while navigating several complex requirements.

  • Integrate seamlessly with existing landscape and historic home aesthetic
  • Navigate strict township regulations and historic district considerations
  • Coordinate with existing outdoor structures and mature landscaping
  • Accommodate natural slope while creating a level pool experience
  • Deliver resort-quality features within residential context
  • Complete before their annual summer garden party

Our Solution

A comprehensive approach combining premium materials, expert engineering, and meticulous project management.

  • Selected a premium Latham fiberglass shell with custom color to complement stonework
  • Designed infinity edge facing the garden to maximize view lines
  • Integrated a raised spa with spillover creating a soothing water feature
  • Engineered custom retaining solution to work with natural grade
  • Coordinated closely with landscape architect for seamless integration
  • Implemented accelerated schedule with dedicated project team

Project Timeline

From groundbreaking to first swim in just 6 weeks

Week 1

Site Preparation & Excavation

Utility marking, protection of existing landscaping, excavation of pool area, and initial grading work completed.

Week 2

Shell Delivery & Setting

Fiberglass shell delivered via specialized transport, crane placement, and precise leveling to specification.

Week 3

Plumbing & Electrical

Installation of all plumbing lines, equipment pad preparation, electrical runs, and bonding work completed.

Week 4

Backfill & Equipment

Controlled backfill process, equipment installation (pump, filter, heater, salt system), and infinity edge catch basin.

Week 5

Decking & Coping

Travertine coping installation, deck preparation, and premium paver installation around pool perimeter.

Week 6

Final Details & Startup

Landscape restoration, final grading, pool fill, water chemistry balancing, client orientation, and final inspection.

Key Takeaways

Lessons and insights from this luxury installation

Premium Materials Matter

The investment in travertine decking and premium fiberglass shell pays dividends in both aesthetics and longevity. Quality materials reduce long-term maintenance and enhance property value.

Early Coordination is Critical

Working closely with the client's landscape architect from day one ensured seamless integration. The pool enhances the garden design rather than competing with it.

Fiberglass Enables Speed

A comparable concrete pool would have taken 12-16 weeks. Fiberglass construction allowed us to deliver luxury features in half the time without compromising quality.

Communication Builds Trust

Daily photo updates and weekly on-site meetings kept clients informed and confident. Transparency about timeline and any challenges strengthened the relationship.
