What We Repair

Equipment We Service

As pool builders and maintenance experts, we understand every component of your pool system and can diagnose and repair issues quickly.

Pumps & Motors

Repair or replacement of pool pumps, variable speed motors, and circulation systems. We work with all major brands.

Filters

Sand, cartridge, and DE filter repair and maintenance. Valve replacement, media changes, and pressure issues.

Heaters

Gas, electric, and heat pump heater repair. Ignition issues, heat exchanger problems, and thermostat calibration.

Salt Systems

Salt chlorine generator repair and cell replacement. Calibration, flow sensor issues, and control board repair.

Automation

Pool control system troubleshooting and repair. Pentair, Hayward, and Jandy systems. App connectivity issues.

Lights & Electrical

LED light replacement, transformer repair, and GFCI troubleshooting. All electrical work to code.
