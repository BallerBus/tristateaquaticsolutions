FAQ

Common Questions

How long does fiberglass pool installation take?

Most fiberglass pool installations are completed in 2-4 weeks from groundbreaking to first swim. The pool shell itself is typically set in just one day. The remaining time is for excavation, plumbing, electrical, decking, and finishing work.

How much does a fiberglass pool cost?

Fiberglass pool installations typically range from $60,000-$100,000 in the Main Line PA and Delaware area. This includes the pool shell, installation, basic decking, equipment, and permits. Final cost depends on pool size, features, and site conditions.

What are the advantages of fiberglass over concrete?

Fiberglass pools offer faster installation (2-4 weeks vs 8-12 weeks), lower maintenance costs, smoother non-porous surfaces that resist algae, reduced chemical usage, and no resurfacing requirements. They also come with lifetime structural warranties.

What warranty comes with a fiberglass pool?

Our fiberglass pools include lifetime structural warranties from manufacturers. This covers the pool shell against structural defects for as long as you own the pool. Equipment warranties range from 1-3 years depending on the component.

Can fiberglass pools have custom shapes?

Fiberglass pools come in a wide variety of pre-designed shapes and sizes from leading manufacturers. While not fully custom like concrete, there are hundreds of options including geometric, freeform, and sport pools in sizes from 12 to 40+ feet.

Do you handle permits for installation?

Yes, we handle the complete permit process including applications, drawings, and inspections. Permit requirements vary by township in PA and DE, and our team is familiar with local requirements throughout our service area.
