Types of Heaters

Heaters We Service

Our technicians are trained and experienced with all major types of pool heating systems.

Gas Heaters

Natural gas and propane pool heaters. We diagnose and repair ignition systems, heat exchangers, gas valves, thermostats, and control boards. Fast heating for any pool size.

Heat Pumps

Electric heat pump repair and service. Compressor issues, refrigerant leaks, fan motors, defrost cycles, and control systems. Energy-efficient heating solutions.

Electric Heaters

Electric resistance heater repair. Heating elements, thermostats, high-limit switches, and electrical connections. Ideal for spas and smaller pools.

Solar Heating Systems

Solar panel and collector repair. Valve actuators, control systems, flow sensors, and panel leaks. Eco-friendly pool heating maintenance.

FAQ

Heater Repair Questions

Common questions about pool heater repair and service.

How much does pool heater repair cost?

Pool heater repair costs typically range from $150 to $600 depending on the issue. Simple fixes like thermostat calibration or pilot light repairs are on the lower end, while heat exchanger repairs or control board replacements are more expensive. We provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

Why is my pool heater not heating the water?

Common causes include ignition failure, clogged burners, low gas pressure, faulty thermostats, dirty filters restricting flow, or heat exchanger issues. For heat pumps, low refrigerant or fan motor problems are common culprits. Our technicians diagnose the exact cause during the service call.

How long do pool heaters typically last?

Gas pool heaters typically last 5-10 years with proper maintenance. Heat pumps generally last 10-15 years since they have fewer combustion components. Regular maintenance including annual inspections can extend the life of your heater significantly.

Should I repair or replace my old pool heater?

As a general rule, if repair costs exceed 50% of replacement cost and the heater is over 7 years old, replacement is often more economical. Newer heaters are also significantly more energy efficient. We'll give you an honest assessment and help you make the best decision for your situation.

Do you service all brands of pool heaters?

Yes, we service all major pool heater brands including Pentair, Hayward, Raypak, Jandy, and Sta-Rite. Our technicians are trained and experienced with both gas heaters and heat pumps from all manufacturers.
