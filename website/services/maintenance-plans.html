FAQ

Common Questions

How often should my pool be maintained?

For optimal water quality and equipment health, weekly professional maintenance is recommended during swimming season (typically May through September in PA/DE). This ensures consistent chemical balance and catches potential issues early.

What does weekly maintenance include?

Our weekly service includes skimming, vacuuming, brushing walls, testing and balancing chemicals, emptying baskets, inspecting equipment, and providing a detailed service report. Higher tier plans include additional services.

Do I need to be home during service?

No, you do not need to be home. As long as we have gate access to the pool area, we can complete service while you are away. You will receive a detailed service report via email after each visit.

When should I open and close my pool?

In the PA/DE region, pool opening typically occurs late April to mid-May, and closing happens late September to mid-October. Timing depends on weather and your personal preference. We recommend booking early for best scheduling.

Are chemicals included in maintenance plans?

Yes, all necessary chemicals for water balancing are included in our maintenance plans. This includes chlorine, pH adjusters, algaecides, and shock treatments as needed.
