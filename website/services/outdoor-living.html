FAQ

Common Questions

Do you design outdoor living spaces?

Yes, we provide complete outdoor living design including 3D renderings. We coordinate all aspects of your backyard transformation from pool to patio to landscaping, ensuring a cohesive, beautiful result.

What materials do you use for pool decking?

We offer concrete (stamped, brushed, colored), pavers (natural stone, travertine, porcelain), and composite decking. Material selection depends on budget, style preferences, and maintenance requirements.

Can you build an outdoor kitchen?

Yes, we design and build complete outdoor kitchens including grills, cooktops, refrigerators, sinks, and bars. Custom cabinetry and countertops are available in various materials including granite, concrete, and stainless steel.

Do you handle landscaping?

We coordinate landscaping as part of complete projects. This includes grading, drainage, plantings, and lawn restoration. We work with trusted landscape partners for specialized plantings and ongoing landscape maintenance.
