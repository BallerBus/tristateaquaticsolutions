FAQ

Common Questions

Can I add a spa to my existing pool?

Yes, spas can be added to most existing pools. Options include attached spillover spas that share the pool's equipment or separate standalone spas with independent heating and filtration systems.

How much do LED pool lights cost?

LED pool light installation typically costs $1,500-$4,000 depending on the number of lights, existing wiring, and automation integration. LED lights last 10+ years and offer dramatic color-changing options.

What is pool automation?

Pool automation allows you to control pumps, heaters, lights, and other equipment from your smartphone or tablet. You can schedule operations, adjust temperature, and monitor your pool remotely from anywhere.

Can you add a heater to my pool?

Yes, we install gas heaters, heat pumps, and solar heating systems. Heaters extend your swimming season significantly and can be controlled automatically or remotely through automation systems.
