FAQ

Common Questions

How much does pool renovation cost?

Pool renovation costs range from $15,000 for basic resurfacing to $100,000+ for complete remodels with new equipment, tile, decking, and features. We provide detailed quotes during your free consultation.

How long does pool renovation take?

Simple resurfacing takes 1-2 weeks. Full renovations with equipment replacement and feature additions take 4-8 weeks depending on scope.

Can you add features to my existing pool?

Yes! We can add spas, water features, tanning ledges, LED lighting, automation, and more to most existing pools. Some features require structural modifications which we assess during consultation.

Is it worth renovating an old pool?

In most cases, yes. Renovation costs 30-50% less than building new and can transform an aging pool into a modern backyard centerpiece with updated finishes and energy-efficient equipment.
