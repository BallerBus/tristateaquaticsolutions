What We Test

Comprehensive Water Analysis

We test for every parameter that affects your water quality, swimmer comfort, and equipment longevity.

pH Level

The foundation of water balance. Affects sanitizer effectiveness, swimmer comfort, and equipment protection.

Ideal: 7.2 - 7.6

Chlorine / Bromine

Your primary sanitizer. Kills bacteria and algae to keep swimmers safe and water clear.

Ideal: 1.0 - 3.0 ppm

Total Alkalinity

The pH buffer that prevents wild swings in acidity. Keeps your water stable and predictable.

Ideal: 80 - 120 ppm

Calcium Hardness

Protects your surfaces and equipment. Too low causes etching, too high causes scale.

Ideal: 200 - 400 ppm

Cyanuric Acid (CYA)

Chlorine stabilizer that protects against UV degradation. Essential for outdoor pools.

Ideal: 30 - 50 ppm

Phosphates & Metals

Hidden contaminants that cause algae blooms and staining. Professional testing catches what home kits miss.

Ideal: < 100 ppb
Why It Matters

The Science of Perfect Water

Pool chemistry is interconnected. Change one parameter and others shift. That is why guesswork leads to problems and wasted chemicals.

  • Swimmer safety. Properly balanced water prevents skin irritation, red eyes, and protects against harmful bacteria.
  • Equipment protection. Imbalanced water corrodes pumps, heaters, and surfaces, leading to expensive repairs.
  • Chemical efficiency. Balanced water uses less chemicals. Save money while getting better results.
  • Visual clarity. When chemistry is right, water sparkles. No haze, no algae, no cloudy days.
