What's Included

Each weekly visit follows our comprehensive checklist to ensure nothing is missed.

  • Surface SkimmingRemove leaves, bugs, and floating debris from the water surface.
  • VacuumingClean pool floor and remove settled debris and sediment.
  • Brush Walls & TilesPrevent algae buildup on walls, steps, and waterline tile.

FAQ

Common Questions

How often should my pool be professionally maintained?

For optimal water quality and equipment health, weekly professional maintenance is recommended during swimming season (typically May through September in the PA/DE region). This ensures consistent chemical balance and catches potential issues early.

What day of the week will you service my pool?

We assign a regular service day based on your location and our routing. We try to accommodate preferences but maintain consistent routes for efficiency. You will always receive service on the same day each week.

Do I need to be home during service?

No, you do not need to be home. As long as we have gate access to the pool area, we can complete service while you are away. You will receive a detailed service report via email after each visit.
