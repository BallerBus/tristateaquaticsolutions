Pool Sizing Tips

Things to consider when choosing your pool size

Setback Requirements

Most areas require 5-10 feet between the pool and property lines. Check local codes before finalizing size.

Leave Room for Decking

Plan for at least 4-6 feet of deck space around the pool for lounging, furniture, and safe movement.

Think Long-Term

Consider future needs - growing family, aging parents, or evolving entertainment style. Bigger is often better.
