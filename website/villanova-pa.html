Common Questions

Pool Installation FAQs for Villanova

Answers to questions we frequently hear from Villanova and Main Line homeowners.

How long does pool installation take in Villanova?

Fiberglass pools are typically installed in 2-4 weeks. Concrete pools take 8-12 weeks depending on complexity. We coordinate all Radnor Township permits to ensure a smooth process.

Do you handle permits for Radnor Township?

Yes. We manage the entire permitting process with Radnor Township, including zoning applications, safety barrier requirements, and final inspections. You don't need to visit township offices.

What's the best pool type for a Villanova estate?

For larger properties and custom designs, concrete pools offer maximum flexibility. For faster installation with excellent durability, fiberglass is ideal. We'll help you choose based on your specific property and goals.

Do you offer pool maintenance services?

Yes. Every installation includes 1-2 years of complimentary maintenance. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your pool pristine year-round.
